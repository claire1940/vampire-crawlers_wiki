## How to Upgrade Skills and Masteries

One of the most common points of confusion for new players is how to actually improve their abilities. Unlike traditional RPGs where skills level up simply by use, Tensura Incremental ties skill power directly to your race's evolution stage. To effectively move up the **tensura incremental skill tier list**, you must follow a specific sequence of upgrades.

First, you must reach 100% Skill Mastery. This is achieved through repeated use of the skill or by using specific training items found in dungeons. However, reaching 100% mastery does not automatically "level up" the skill's tier. Instead, it prepares the skill for a breakthrough.

Once mastery is maxed, the actual upgrade occurs when you **Evolve your race**. Evolution acts as a multiplier for all mastered skills, transforming them into more potent versions. For example, a mastered "Water Blade" may evolve into a "Water Pressure Blade" or similar variant once the player transitions to a higher racial tier.

## Race Evolution and Prestige Strategy

Progression in Tensura Incremental is not a linear path of maxing every single stat. Many players fall into the trap of trying to max out every skill before moving to the next stage of the game. Expert players have discovered that the most efficient way to progress is to focus on your **Race Upgrade**.

The prestige system in Tensura Incremental allows you to reset certain aspects of your progress in exchange for permanent power multipliers. While it might be tempting to stay at a lower level to "perfect" a specific build, the bonuses granted by evolving your race often outweigh the benefits of fully maxing every minor skill.

## Top Skills for Dungeon Clearing

Dungeons are the primary source of items and prestige currency. To clear them efficiently, your loadout should balance AOE (Area of Effect) damage with survivability. According to the current **tensura incremental skill tier list**, the following combinations are most effective for 2026 dungeon runs:

1.  **The Vampire Build:** Combines *Beelzebuth* with *Self-Regeneration*. This allows you to dive into large mobs of enemies and heal back the damage you take almost instantly.
2.  **The Burn Build:** Uses *Black Flame* and *Magic Sense*. This build is designed for kiting bosses, allowing you to see them through walls and obstacles while their health ticks away from fire damage.
3.  **The Speedster Build:** Focuses on *Raphael* and *Water Blade*. By reducing cooldowns to their absolute minimum, you can fire off projectiles in rapid succession, clearing corridors before enemies can reach you.

## Essential Tips for New Players

To make the most of your time in [Tensura Incremental on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/), keep these strategies in mind:

*   **Focus on One Skill Initially:** It is better to have one skill at 100% mastery to trigger a race evolution than to have five skills at 20% mastery.
*   **Check Dungeon Drops:** Certain dungeons have a higher drop rate for "Mastery Scrolls." Use these on your S-Tier skills to skip the manual grind.
*   **Prestige Early:** Don't be afraid to prestige. The initial loss of levels is quickly compensated for by the massive increase in experience gain and damage.
*   **Join a Community:** Since info can be scarce, joining a Discord or guild can provide real-time updates on hidden skill interactions or bug fixes.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to climb the tensura incremental skill tier list?**

A: The fastest way is to focus on race evolution. By evolving your race, you unlock higher tiers of existing skills, automatically moving them from lower ranks to S or A tier status. Always ensure your primary skill is at 100% mastery before hitting the evolution button.

**Q: Do I need to max out every skill before I prestige?**

A: No. In fact, trying to max out every skill before your first few prestiges is highly inefficient. Focus on maxing your race upgrade first, as this provides the largest permanent boost to your character's power.

**Q: Why isn't my skill leveling up even though I use it constantly?**

A: Skills in Tensura Incremental have a mastery cap. Once you hit 100% mastery, the skill will not get any stronger until you evolve your race. Check your character menu to see if you have met the evolution requirements.

**Q: Which race is the best for a magic-focused build?**

A: Currently, the Spirit and Demon paths are highly favored for magic builds due to their natural affinity for high-tier elemental skills and faster mana regeneration rates.
