## How to Evolve and Upgrade Skills

A common mistake many players make is rushing the evolution button as soon as it glows. In Tensura Incremental, the quality of your class is directly tied to your skill mastery. Before you commit to a new race evolution, you must ensure your current kit is fully optimized.

Follow these essential steps for skill progression:
1.  **Achieve 100% Skill Mastery**: Never evolve your race until your current active skills have reached 100% mastery. Skipping this step often results in weaker skill versions in your next form.
2.  **Evolve Your Race**: The act of evolving is what actually triggers the upgrade for your skills.
3.  **Focus on Race Upgrades**: While it is tempting to try and max out every individual skill, your primary focus should be on your Race Upgrade level to unlock higher prestige tiers.

## Dungeon Strategies for High-Tier Classes

To reach the top of the **tensura incremental class tier list**, you will need to farm specific dungeons for evolution materials. Each class tier has a recommended dungeon level to ensure you aren't wasting time on low-reward content.

*   **Early Game (C-Tier)**: Focus on the Forest Dungeons. These provide the basic essence needed for the first three evolutions.
*   **Mid Game (B-Tier to A-Tier)**: Move to the Cave and Orc Disaster zones. Here, you will find the materials required for Ogre and Kijin transitions.
*   **Late Game (S-Tier)**: The Spirit Dwelling and Demon Lord's Banquet dungeons are mandatory for those seeking the True Demon Lord title.

> **Tip:** Always check the [Official Roblox Group](https://www.roblox.com/groups/1) or community hubs for active codes, as these often provide 2x Mastery boosts which are vital for quick class transitions.

## Understanding the Evolution Tree

The complexity of the evolution tree is what makes the **tensura incremental class tier list** so dynamic. Depending on your starting race, your path to the S-Tier will vary. 

### The Monster Path
Starting as a Slime or Goblin is the most popular route. This path leads through the Direwolf and Ogre lineages, eventually branching into the Kijin or Fair Oni. This path is favored by players who prefer high physical damage and fast attack speeds.

### The Demonic Path
Starting as a Lesser Demon offers a more magic-focused experience. While the early game can be slightly slower due to longer cooldowns, the transition into a Greater Demon and eventually an Arch Demon provides some of the best AOE (Area of Effect) capabilities in the game.

### The Human Path
Often overlooked, the Human path leads to the Saint and Hero classes. These classes excel in survivability and are often the best choice for players who enjoy soloing difficult boss dungeons without the need for a dedicated party.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to climb the tensura incremental class tier list?**

A: The fastest way to climb is to focus on 100% skill mastery before every evolution and prioritizing race upgrades over individual skill levels. Using Mastery Boosts during the "Ogre" phase can significantly cut down the mid-game grind.

**Q: Can I change my class path after I have started?**

A: Yes, but it typically requires a prestige reset or a specific "Rebirth" item. It is generally better to finish your current evolution line to gain the permanent prestige bonuses before switching to a new starting race.

**Q: Why are my skills not upgrading when I evolve?**

A: Skills only upgrade if they have reached 100% mastery in your previous form. If you evolve at 90% mastery, the skill will remain at its current tier, missing out on the power spike associated with the new class.

**Q: Is the True Demon Lord class worth the grind?**

A: Absolutely. It offers the highest base multipliers in the game and unlocks "Ultimate Skills" that are required to clear the 2026 endgame raids effectively. It is the undisputed king of any **tensura incremental class tier list**.
