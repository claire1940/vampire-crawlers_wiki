## Understanding the Prestige System

Prestige is where the "incremental" part of the game truly shines. Once you reach a certain threshold, resetting your progress grants you permanent buffs that make subsequent runs significantly faster. 

As of the 2026 updates, the prestige system has been revamped to include "Soul Echoes." These echoes can be spent on a dedicated talent tree that persists through all future resets. 

### Prestige Priority Checklist
1. **Max out Race Upgrades:** Do not prestige until you have reached at least the third stage of your current race evolution.
2. **Bank Magicules:** The amount of Soul Echoes you receive is directly tied to your current Magicule count at the time of reset.
3. **Equip Luck Spirits:** Before hitting the prestige button, ensure you have your best Luck-based spirits equipped to potentially proc a "Critical Reset," which doubles your rewards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid prestiging too early. If you haven't reached at least level 500 in your current run, the bonus multipliers won't be enough to overcome the loss of your current skills.

## Advanced Progression Strategies

To stay at the top of the **tensura incremental tier list**, you must optimize your idle time. Tensura Incremental allows for significant offline progress, but only if you set your "Auto-Skill" priority correctly.

1. **Focus on Multipliers first:** In your skill tree, prioritize anything that says "Global Multiplier" over flat stat increases.
2. **Dungeon Farming:** Use your strongest AOE (Area of Effect) skills to clear low-level dungeons in bulk. The item drop rates are more favorable when you can clear 10+ rooms per minute.
3. **Code Redemption:** Developers frequently release codes that provide temporary 2x Magicule boosts. Always save these for when you are about to push for a new evolution.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best starting race in Tensura Incremental?**

A: For most players, the Slime race is the best starting choice. While it starts weak, its evolution path into "Demon Slime" provides the most versatile stat spread and the highest late-game potential on the **tensura incremental tier list**.

**Q: How do I use codes in Tensura Incremental?**

A: To use codes, look for the "Settings" or "Twitter" icon on the left side of your screen. Enter the working 2026 codes there to receive boosts, Magicules, and sometimes exclusive spirits.

**Q: Do I need to max all skills before I evolve?**

A: No. As discovered by top players, you only need to meet the specific "Race Upgrade" requirements to progress to the next evolution. Maxing every skill is a waste of time in the early to mid-game.

**Q: Which spirit is best for idle farming?**

A: The Greater Light Spirit is currently the best for idle farming due to its massive +500% Magicule gain. If you are focusing on active play and dungeon clearing, the Abyssal Darkness spirit is a better choice for the damage boost.
