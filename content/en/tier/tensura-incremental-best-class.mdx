## The Aura System and Combat Mechanics

The core of your power in Tensura Incremental is your **Aura**. Aura acts as both a level gate and a direct damage multiplier. As seen in recent community tests, a player with 35,000 Aura deals significantly more damage to NPCs than a player with only 10,000 Aura, regardless of their equipped gear.

### How to Maximize Aura Gains
1.  **Dungeon Speedruns:** Completing a dungeon in under three minutes provides a 20% Aura bonus.
2.  **Meditation Skills:** Equipping passive meditation skills allows you to gain Aura even while away from the keyboard (AFK).
3.  **PvP Invading:** Successfully invading a dungeon and defeating the host allows you to "leech" a portion of their earned Aura for that run.

For many, the **tensura incremental best class** for farming Aura is the Assassin, specifically because of the speed at which they can clear mobs. However, the Tank class allows for "Bravery Farming," where you can pull entire rooms of enemies and defeat them with area-of-effect (AoE) counter-attacks.

## Mastering the Invading Mechanic

One of the most controversial yet rewarding features in 2026 is the Dungeon Invading system. Once you unlock your second class advancement, you gain the ability to enter a dungeon that is already being cleared by another player.

If you are playing the **tensura incremental best class** for PvP (the Assassin), invading is a lucrative way to gain resources. However, it carries a high risk; if the host or their party defeats you, you will lose a percentage of your current Aura. 

To successfully defend against an invader, it is recommended to have at least one Tank in your party. Tanks can use "Taunt" skills to force the invader to target them, allowing the DPS members of the party to safely eliminate the threat.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute tensura incremental best class for a solo player?**

A: For solo players, the **Holy Knight (Tank Path)** is generally considered the best. While it may take slightly longer to clear dungeons compared to an Assassin, the high survivability ensures that you rarely fail a run or lose progress due to death. It is the most reliable path for steady progression.

**Q: How do I unlock the secret "Dragonoid" class?**

A: The Dragonoid is a hidden evolution that requires you to reach the "True Demon Lord" tier while maintaining a specific balance of stats. You must have exactly equal amounts of Strength and Agility when you trigger the evolution crystal. It is currently one of the strongest classes in the game but is very difficult to build correctly.

**Q: Can I change my class if I don't like my current playstyle?**

A: Yes, but it is not recommended during the mid-game. You will need a "Class Reset Scroll," which can be purchased from the premium shop or found as a very rare drop from S-Rank World Bosses. Using a scroll will reset your class level to 1, but you will keep your racial evolution and total Aura.

**Q: Does the "Aura" system affect my class skills?**

A: Absolutely. Most skills in Tensura Incremental have "Aura Scaling." This means that as your total Aura increases, the base damage and utility of your class skills grow exponentially. This is why focusing on Aura gain is often more important than finding the perfect piece of equipment in the early stages of the game.
