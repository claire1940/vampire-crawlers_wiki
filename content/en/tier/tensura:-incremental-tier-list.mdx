## Understanding Skill Evolution and Mastery

One of the most common mistakes new players make is ignoring the Mastery system. To climb the **tensura: incremental tier list**, you must understand that skills do not simply level up through use alone. There is a two-step process to truly unlocking a skill's power.

First, you must reach **100% Skill Mastery**. This is achieved by repeatedly using the skill in combat or during training sessions. However, reaching 100% is only half the battle. To actually "upgrade" the skill into a more powerful version (such as turning a basic "Water Blade" into a "Flare Circle"), you must perform a **Race Evolution**.

## Effective Progression Strategy for 2026

To move up the **tensura: incremental tier list** efficiently, you should follow a structured progression loop. Incremental games are all about stacking multipliers, and Tensura is no different.

1.  **Focus on Mastery Early**: Do not rush into dungeons until your primary attacking skill has reached at least 80% mastery. This ensures you aren't wasting time on low-damage output.
2.  **Prioritize Evolution**: As soon as you meet the requirements for a race evolution, take it. Evolution resets some minor stats but significantly boosts your "Growth Rate," which is the most important stat for long-term play.
3.  **Equip Dungeon Items**: Dungeons are the primary source of equipment that provides flat stat boosts. These flat boosts are then multiplied by your race and skill bonuses.
4.  **Use Codes Wisely**: Save your "Magicule Boost" codes for when you are about to perform a major evolution to speed up the recovery process.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Attempting to evolve your race before reaching 100% mastery on your core skills can sometimes lead to "locked" skill slots, making it harder to upgrade them later. Always max your mastery first.
