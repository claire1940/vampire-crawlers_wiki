### Mastering Skill Upgrades
One of the most frequent questions appearing in community discussions involves the evolution of skills. Skills do not "auto-level" once they hit 100% mastery. Instead, the mastery acts as a prerequisite. 

1.  **Fill the Bar:** Use the skill repeatedly until it reaches 100%.
2.  **Gather Materials:** Collect the necessary souls and evolution stones.
3.  **Trigger Evolution:** Click the "Evolve" button in the character menu. The skill will only transform if the mastery was capped *before* the evolution animation began.

> **Warning:** Never attempt a race evolution if your primary combat skills are at 99% mastery. Doing so will lock those skills in their lower forms for the remainder of your current prestige cycle, significantly hampering your DPS.

## Advanced Progression & Mastery Tricks

Once you have mastered the basics, you can employ high-level strategies to shave days off your prestige climb. These "pro-tips" are often discovered by the community shortly after a new **tensura incremental update log** is published.

### The "Mastery Trick"
To farm skill mastery quickly, you need to hit targets as often as possible. Use low-damage, high-hit-count skills on bosses with massive health pools. This allows you to generate mastery points without killing the boss too quickly, effectively turning a single encounter into a massive mastery farm.

### Soul Management and Evolution Breakthroughs
Don't spend your souls on minor stat boosts like "+1% Health." Instead, hoard them for the "Evolution Breakthrough" which occurs every 10 racial tiers. These breakthroughs require a massive influx of souls but provide the largest stat jumps in the game.

### Dungeon Skipping
If your DPS is more than 10x the requirement for your current floor, check the UI for the "Skip" toggle. This allows you to bypass trash mobs and jump directly to the boss room. This is the fastest way to farm specific boss drops and materials for your next evolution.

## FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my skill evolving after a Race Evolution?**

A: This usually happens if the skill did not reach 100% mastery before you clicked the evolution button. Check the **tensura incremental update log** for your specific version, as some updates change the mastery requirements for secret or "Hidden Path" races.

**Q: Do I need to max every skill before I prestige?**

A: No. In the 2026 build, you only need to reach the maximum Race Upgrade level for your current tier. Maxing every skill is purely optional and only recommended if you are struggling to clear a specific dungeon boss.

**Q: What is the fastest way to farm Mythic items?**

A: Find the highest floor you can "one-shot" and enable the "Skip" toggle to focus entirely on the boss. Efficiency (items per hour) is always better than clearing a difficult floor that takes several minutes to finish.

**Q: Are there hidden races in the latest update?**
