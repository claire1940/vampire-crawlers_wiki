## How to Unlock Ultimate Skills

Unlocking an Ultimate Skill is a multi-stage process that requires significant grinding and resource management. You cannot simply roll for these skills; they must be earned through evolution and mastery. Follow these steps to unlock your first Ultimate:

1.  **Max Out Unique Skills:** Ensure all your Unique Skills have reached 100% mastery.
2.  **Evolve Your Race:** Once mastery is capped, proceed with your racial evolution.
3.  **Purchase the Ultimate Upgrade:** After evolving, you will see a second upgrade option. This costs **10,000 Souls**.
4.  **Increase Magic Capacity:** Ultimate Skills require between 2 million and 4 million magic to cast. You must farm Existence Points (EP) to raise your cap.

> **Warning:** Attempting to evolve before reaching 100% mastery on your skills may result in a weaker build. Always verify your mastery percentages in the skill menu before spending souls.

## Mastering Skill Upgrades and Evolution

A common point of confusion for new players is how to actually upgrade the tier of their skills. In Tensura Incremental, skills do not level up simply by using them; they level up when you evolve your character's race.

### The Skill Mastery Loop
To successfully upgrade a skill, you must first reach 100% mastery. Mastery is gained by using the skill in combat against enemies or bosses. Once the skill reaches its cap, it is "primed" for evolution. When you perform a racial evolution, the primed skills will automatically transition to their next tier (e.g., Basic to Unique, or Unique to Ultimate).

> **💡 Tip:** Use the training area to safely grind mastery on new skills before taking them into high-level raids or dungeons.

## Existence Points and Magic Scaling

With Ultimate Skills requiring millions of magic points, farming Existence Points (EP) has become the primary endgame activity. EP acts as a measure of your overall power and directly influences your maximum magic pool.

### Best Ways to Farm EP:
*   **Raids:** Participating in raids provides the highest concentration of EP and souls. Ensure you are part of a balanced party with at least one player using **Lord of Purity** for sustain.
*   **Training:** For solo players, the training grounds offer a steady, albeit slower, stream of EP.
*   **Dungeon Floors:** Clearing higher floors in the dungeon provides a mix of EP, items, and rare drops.

The **tensura incremental new update** has made it so that your magic capacity is the primary bottleneck for power. Even if you have the strongest Ultimate Skill, it is useless if you cannot meet the 2-4 million magic requirement. Focus your early-game resources on increasing your EP before worrying about the 10,000 soul cost for Ultimates.

The new UI provides better visibility into your cooldowns and the ultimate meter. In combat, your rotation should focus on maximizing basic skill usage to keep your ultimate meter charging.
