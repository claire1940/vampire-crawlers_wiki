## How to Unlock Ultimate Skills

Unlocking Ultimate Skills is the centerpiece of the 2026 update. These are not handed out easily; they require a significant investment of time, mastery, and resources. Unlike Unique skills, which serve as your primary toolkit for the mid-game, Ultimates represent the pinnacle of your character's power.

To begin the process of unlocking an Ultimate, you must follow a specific progression path. Skipping any of these steps will prevent the "Evolve" option from appearing in your skill menu.

### Step 1: Maximize Unique Skill Mastery
Before you can even think about Ultimates, you must reach **100% Mastery** on your current Unique skills. This is achieved through consistent use in combat. Farming low-level mobs is a viable way to grind mastery, but participating in high-level raids provides a significantly higher mastery gain per minute.

### Step 2: Racial Evolution
Once your mastery is capped, you must evolve your race. This usually requires a specific level threshold and the completion of certain milestones within the game's story or progression quests.

### Step 3: The Soul Investment
The final and most expensive step is the second racial upgrade. This specific upgrade costs **10,000 Souls**. Souls are a premium currency earned through defeating bosses and completing difficult challenges. Once this investment is made, the Ultimate Skills associated with your path will become available for use.

> **Warning:** Ensure you have chosen the correct evolution path before spending your 10,000 souls, as resetting your racial progression can be extremely costly in the current 2026 meta.

## Ultimate Skill Mechanics and Charging

Using an Ultimate Skill is not as simple as clicking a button. It requires careful management of your **Ultimate Meter** and your **Magic** reserves. 

### Charging the Ultimate Meter
The Ultimate Meter is a new mechanic introduced alongside the **tensura: incremental ultimates and new ui** update. To fill this meter, you must engage in combat using **Basic Skills only**. 
*   **Racial Skills:** Do not charge the meter.
*   **Spirit Skills:** Do not charge the meter.
*   **Unique Skills:** Do not charge the meter.

This design choice forces players to balance their high-damage Unique skills with Basic attacks to ensure their Ultimate is ready for the final phase of a boss fight.

### Magic Requirements
Even with a full meter, Ultimate Skills have a massive Magic cost. Most Ultimates require between **2 million and 4 million Magic** to activate. If your Magic pool is too small, the skill will remain greyed out even if the meter is full. To increase your maximum Magic, you must farm **Existence Points (EP)**.

| **Elite Mob Farming** | Medium | Moderate |
| **World Boss Raids** | High | Hard |
| **Timed Challenges** | Very High | Expert |
