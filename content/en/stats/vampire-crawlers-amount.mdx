## The Combo System: Numerical Strategy

One of the most innovative features in this spin-off is the numerical combo system. Every card in your hand has a mana cost, typically ranging from 0 to 3. By playing cards in ascending numerical order (e.g., playing a 0-mana card, then a 1-mana card, then a 2-mana card), you trigger a combo chain.

The **vampire crawlers amount** of damage dealt increases exponentially with each successful step in the combo. This system rewards players for holding onto low-cost cards to "bridge" into their heavy-hitting 3-mana finishers. It also applies to buffs; a Spinach card played at the end of a long combo chain provides a significantly higher stat boost than if it were played individually.
