## Combat Mechanics: Mana and Knockback

Combat in Vampire Crawlers is a delicate balance of mana management. You typically start with 2 or 3 mana, meaning you can only play a few high-impact cards per turn. To maintain your **vampire crawlers above 100 hp**, you must use knockback mechanics to your advantage.

The **King Bible** and **Axe** cards have a high chance to knock enemies back. When an enemy is pushed back, they must spend their next turn moving forward rather than attacking. This effectively "heals" you by preventing damage from occurring in the first place. Expert players often prioritize knockback over raw damage in the early game to preserve their health pool for the final boss.

### Gem Slotting for Durability

The Gem Hammer is a mid-game unlock that allows you to add gems to your existing cards. This is a game-changer for defensive builds.

*   **Might Gems:** Increase the damage of your defensive weapons like the King Bible.
*   **Area Gems:** Increase the range of your knockback, protecting more of the screen.
*   **Amount Gems:** Allow weapons to hit more targets, which is vital when surrounded by ghosts.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when fighting explosive ghosts. These enemies deal damage to you upon death. If you kill a group of them simultaneously with an AoE attack, you can lose 20-30 HP instantly, even if you have **vampire crawlers above 100 hp**.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I permanently increase my health in Vampire Crawlers?**

A: Permanent health increases are primarily achieved through the Hollow Heart card. Additionally, certain character power-ups purchased in the Town Hall using gold can provide a permanent boost to your starting HP, making it easier to stay **vampire crawlers above 100 hp** in future runs.

**Q: What is the best character for a high-HP build?**

A: Antonio is excellent for beginners due to his high starting health and armor synergies. However, Imelda is often preferred by advanced players because her XP growth allows her to find health-scaling cards much faster than other characters.

**Q: Does armor stack with health?**

A: Yes. Armor reduces the damage you take from each individual hit, while health is your total pool. Having high armor makes every point of health in your **vampire crawlers above 100 hp** build significantly more valuable, as it takes more enemy hits to deplete your pool.

**Q: Can I heal during a battle?**

A: Healing during battle is difficult and usually requires specific cards or finding breakable objects like braziers that drop food. Some rare gems can also add life-steal effects to your weapons, though these are difficult to find in the early stages of the game.
