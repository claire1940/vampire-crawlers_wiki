## Essential Cards for Your Collection

Regardless of the **vampire crawlers demo carry over to full game** status, mastering these cards is vital for reaching the final floor and defeating Death.

### The Bracer (Hand Size King)
The Bracer is a 2-mana card that increases your hand size by one for the rest of the fight. In a game where your deck is your lifeblood, drawing more cards is the most powerful buff available. When combined with mana-generating cards like the **Light Tome**, you can eventually draw your entire deck every single turn.

### Pichon (The Spicy Pigeon)
Pichon is a high-cost weapon (3 mana) that fires multiple projectiles every round. Its true power comes from the "Amount" stat. By slotting gems into your tomes that increase your Amount statistic, you can turn a single pigeon into a swarm that deals millions of damage per second, effectively evaporating bosses before they appear on screen.

### Weighty Tome
This is a rare item that serves as a vessel for upgrades. It is highly recommended to slot a **Return Gem** into the Weighty Tome. This ensures that the card returns to your hand after being played, allowing for infinite mana and armor generation if you have the draw power to support it.
