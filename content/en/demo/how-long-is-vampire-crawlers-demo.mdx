## Content and Stages in the Demo

The primary setting for the demo is the Mad Forest. While this name will be familiar to *Vampire Survivors* veterans, the layout is entirely different. Instead of an open 2D field, you navigate a 3D grid-based dungeon. The demo limits you to one main stage, but it is packed with secrets, destructible objects like logs and coffins, and hidden mana obelisks.

### The Four-Floor Structure
Each run is divided into four floors. As you progress, the enemy density increases, and the "horde" feeling becomes more apparent. On the third and fourth floors, you will encounter elite enemies and eventually the "Flower Boss," which serves as the final challenge of the demo.

### Rescue and Recruitment
One of the most significant features that impacts **how long is Vampire Crawlers demo** is the character rescue system. During your first few runs, your primary goal is to find and rescue Antonio. Once rescued, he joins your "Crawlers" roster, allowing you to use him in future runs. This recruitment system adds layers of replayability as different characters offer unique starting decks and passive abilities.

## Core Gameplay Mechanics

Understanding the mechanics is essential to maximizing your time. Unlike traditional deckbuilders where you might play one or two cards per turn, *Vampire Crawlers* encourages high-volume card play to mimic the "bullet heaven" style.

### The Combo System
The game rewards you for playing cards in a specific order. The **Combo Multiplier** triggers when you play cards in ascending mana cost order (e.g., playing a 0-cost card, then a 1-cost, then a 2-cost). This increases your total damage output for that turn and is vital for clearing large waves of bats and skeletons.

### Gem Hammer and Card Customization
Mid-way through the demo, you will unlock the Gem Hammer. This allows you to socket gems into your cards, adding permanent buffs for the duration of the run. Gems can increase "Might" (damage), "Amount" (number of projectiles), or "Area" (splash damage).

| Gem Type | Effect | Best Card Synergy |
|---|---|---|
| **Might Gem** | Increases base damage | Knife, Axe |
| **Amount Gem** | Adds extra projectiles | Magic Wand, Knife |
| **Area Gem** | Adds splash/cleave damage | King Bible, Whip |
| **Draw Gem** | Draws an extra card on play | 0-Cost cards |

## Meta-Progression and the Town Hall

After a run ends, you return to the Town Hall. This hub area is where you spend the gold earned during your crawls. If you are wondering **how long is Vampire Crawlers demo** for someone who wants to max out their character, the shop is where you will spend the bulk of your time.

Permanent upgrades available in the 2026 demo include:
*   **Duration:** Increases how long "Crawlers" (summons) stay on the field.
*   **Area:** Increases the splash damage radius for all attacks.
*   **Armor:** Provides a flat damage reduction.
*   **Luck:** Increases the chance of finding rare gems and chests.

You can also unlock new characters like Alda, who provides XP bonuses, or purchase the "Bloody Whip" to start your runs with more aggressive options. This meta-progression loop is what keeps the demo engaging for several hours beyond the initial victory.

## Essential Tips for Completing the Demo

To ensure you reach the end of the Mad Forest and defeat the final boss, follow these strategic guidelines:

1.  **Prioritize Mana Obelisks:** Early in the run, look for stone obelisks on the map. Walking into these often grants +1 Max Mana, which is the single most important stat for pulling off long combos.
2.  **Use the "Play All" Feature:** When facing weak enemies, use the "Play All" button (or spacebar) to quickly cycle through your hand. This saves time and keeps the game's pace high.
3.  **Positioning Matters:** Even though it is a card game, your 3D position matters. Some cards, like the Knife, hit enemies directly in front of you, while the King Bible rotates around you. Always face the largest cluster of enemies before playing your hand.
4.  **Destroy Everything:** Break every log, crate, and coffin you see. These contain gold, pancakes (healing), and occasionally rare relics that can define your run.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Flower Boss on Floor 4 has a massive health pool and can quickly overwhelm you if you haven't invested in "Area" or "Amount" upgrades. Ensure your deck has at least one high-damage AoE card before entering the final floor.
