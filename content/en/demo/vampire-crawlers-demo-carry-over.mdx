## Progress Transfer: Does Vampire Crawlers Demo Carry Over?

The most critical question for any dedicated grinder is whether their time spent in the demo is "permanent." In the case of this sequel, the **vampire crawlers demo carry over** is comprehensive. The developers have utilized a unified save system that recognizes demo milestones. This means that when the full version launches later in 2026, you will not have to re-clear the Mad Forest or the Inlaid Library to reach the later stages of the game.

| Data Type | Carry Over Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Gold / Currency** | **Yes** | All gold earned from candles and chests is retained. |
| **Character Unlocks** | **Yes** | Unlocked heroes like Po are available immediately. |
| **Power-up Ranks** | **Yes** | Stat boosts (Health, Splash Damage) persist. |

> **💡 Tip:** Focus your demo time on grinding gold for permanent Power-up Ranks. These provide a 10% boost per rank to health and damage, which is the most valuable asset that carries over to the full game.

## Demo Content and Level Structure

The demo provides a substantial look at the early-game dungeons. Unlike the time-limited maps of the previous entry, these dungeons are floor-based. You must survive a specific number of floors to "complete" the run, though the final boss of the dungeon is often followed by an encounter with Death, which serves as the traditional end-of-run mechanic.

### The Mad Forest
The first map, the Mad Forest, consists of four distinct floors. Each floor increases in difficulty, introducing more "bullet spongy" enemies and complex mob patterns. To successfully trigger the **vampire crawlers demo carry over** for this level's completion, you must defeat the mini-bosses on the final floor.

### The Inlaid Library
The Inlaid Library is significantly more challenging, featuring five floors instead of four. This level introduces more sophisticated environmental hazards and requires a better understanding of the card synergy system.

| Level Name | Floors | Key Boss | Primary Hazard |

## Mastering Card Synergies and Deck Building

The core gameplay loop in *Vampire Crawlers* involves building a deck of attacks and defensive maneuvers during your run. The synergy system is the most innovative addition to the formula. If you play cards in corresponding cost order (e.g., a 0-cost card followed by a 1-cost card), you receive massive compound bonuses.

Follow these steps to maximize your damage output:
1.  **Check Mana Costs:** Always look at the bottom of your card to see the mana requirement.
2.  **Sequence Your Attacks:** Start your turn with a 0-cost card (like the Whip).
3.  **Apply Synergies:** Follow up with a 1-cost card (like the King James Bible) to trigger double damage or increased area of effect.
4.  **Use Blue Cards for Defense:** Blue square cards often represent armor or shields. Playing these can trigger specific character abilities, such as Po's extra card draw.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you play cards out of order (e.g., a 2-cost card then a 0-cost card), the synergy multiplier resets. This can leave you vulnerable during high-stakes boss fights like the Medusa encounter.

## Character Progression and Permanent Unlocks

As you play through the demo, you will unlock new "Crawlers." Each character has a unique starting weapon and a passive ability that changes how you interact with the card deck. Because of the **vampire crawlers demo carry over** system, unlocking these characters early is a top priority.

| Character | Starting Weapon | Unique Ability |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard Hero** | Whip | Balanced stats for beginners. |
| **Po** | **Garlic** | Increases card draw by 1 when played. |
| **Arca** | Fire Wand | Higher chance for "Spicy Damage" modifiers. |

To unlock Po, you generally need to find the Garlic item or reach a specific level milestone within the Mad Forest. Once unlocked, Po is widely considered the best character for farming gold due to the defensive nature of Garlic, which allows you to "walk through" weaker mobs to break candles and collect coins.

## Permanent Power-Ups and Gold Spending

The gold you collect isn't just for show; it is the currency used in the main menu's Power-up shop. Since this gold is part of the **vampire crawlers demo carry over**, you should spend it strategically to make future runs easier.

*   **Max Health:** Increases your survivability by 10% per rank. Essential for the five-floor Library run.
*   **Splash Damage:** Increases the area of effect for weapons like the Axe and Holy Water.
*   **Crawler Duration:** Allows your character to stay in a transformed or "powered up" state for longer periods.
*   **Luck:** Increases the frequency of treasure chests and high-tier card rewards.

## Advanced Demo Tactics: Weapon Evolutions

While the evolutions themselves don't carry over between runs, the knowledge of how to trigger them is vital. In the demo, you can evolve weapons by combining them with specific passive items found in chests or through the vending machines hidden in the dungeons.

1.  **Meteor Strike:** One of the most powerful evolutions in the demo. It clears entire screens and is almost required to defeat the Medusa boss.
2.  **King James Bible (Upgraded):** By applying a "Times Two" damage modifier to the Bible card, you can create a near-impenetrable wall of holy text.
3.  **Rainbow Armor:** This is a rare defensive card that adds massive amounts of armor (up to 18 points). When combined with "Fabulous" modifiers, you become nearly invincible for several turns.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the vampire crawlers demo carry over all my gold to the full game?**

A: Yes, all gold earned during your demo runs is saved to your profile and will be available to spend on power-ups when the full game launches in 2026.

**Q: How do I unlock the Inlaid Library in the demo?**

A: You must successfully complete a full run of the Mad Forest (surviving all 4 floors and defeating the boss) to unlock the Library map.

**Q: What happens when Death appears at the end of a demo run?**

A: Just like in the original game, Death is a scripted "game over" mechanic. Even if you have the Rainbow Armor, Death will eventually one-hit kill you. This still counts as a successful run completion for your progress logs.

**Q: Can I evolve more than one weapon per run in the demo?**

A: Yes, if you collect enough experience to reach high levels (typically level 15+) and find the necessary evolution gems in treasure chests, you can have multiple evolved weapons simultaneously.
