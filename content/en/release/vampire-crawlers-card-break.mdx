## FAQ

**Q: What does it mean when a card looks "shattered" in my hand?**

A: This is a visual representation of a **vampire crawlers card break**. It indicates the card has been played or copied so many times in a single turn that it has reached its maximum potential for that round. It is a sign that your infinite combo is working correctly.

**Q: Can I achieve a card break with any character?**

A: While any character can technically break the deck, characters like Cavalo and Giovanna are preferred in 2026. Cavalo's ability to increase projectiles per level and Giovanna's speed buffs make it much easier to trigger the necessary card draw sequences.

**Q: Why does my combo meter reset even when I use a Wild Card?**

A: Some "Easy Combo" gems have a hidden property that resets the combo to zero in exchange for making the card free. Always check the gem description before applying it to your core loop cards like the Attract Orb.

**Q: How much damage is needed to kill Death in Vampire Crawlers?**

A: You typically need to reach a single-hit damage threshold of at least 30,000 to 35,000. This is only possible by sustaining a card break for several minutes to stack damage multipliers from Spinach and other growth-based cards.
