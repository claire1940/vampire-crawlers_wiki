### The Card System and Console Integration
On console platforms, the game integrates a robust card-based mechanic. These cards represent your skills, weapons, and defensive maneuvers. > **💡 Tip:** Always keep a "Hammer" card in your active hand. It is one of the most versatile tools for environmental interaction and unexpected enemy encounters.

## Character Progression and Strategy

Success in the wake of the **vampire crawlers launch** depends on how you manage your character's growth. Each run provides "a little change" and experience that can be funneled into a massive skill tree.
