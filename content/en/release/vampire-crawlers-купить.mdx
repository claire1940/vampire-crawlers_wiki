## Getting Started: Mechanics and Card Economy

The core of Vampire Crawlers revolves around the mana system and card combos. You typically start a run with two mana, which replenishes each turn. Cards are categorized by their mana cost, ranging from 0-cost utility cards to 4-cost screen-clearers. The most important mechanic to master is the "Combo Multiplier." Every card played in sequence increases your combo, which can significantly boost the damage or projectile count of subsequent cards.

In 2026, the meta has shifted toward "Combo Maintenance" Arcana. Normally, combos reset at the end of a turn, but specific Arcana allow you to carry that momentum forward. This is essential for late-game bosses where a 15x or 20x multiplier is required to pierce through their massive health pools.
