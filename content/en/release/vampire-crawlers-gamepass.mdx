## Map Exploration and Treasure Hunting

Exploration is a key pillar of the experience. Maps are not just infinite looping plains; they contain static landmarks, hidden treasures, and lore-significant altars. ### Treasure Chest Tiers
1. **Bronze Chests**: Found frequently; provide gold and a single weapon upgrade.

3. **Gold Chests**: Hidden in secret map locations; offer a chance for "Turbo Evolutions."
