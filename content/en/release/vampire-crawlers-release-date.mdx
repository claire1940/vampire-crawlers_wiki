## Understanding the "Turbo Turn" Gameplay

The most significant departure from previous entries in the genre is the "Turbo Turn" mechanic. While traditional survivors-like games rely on real-time movement and automatic firing, *Vampire Crawlers* introduces a high-speed, turn-based hybrid. Every "crawl" or step you take counts as a turn, but the "Turbo" aspect ensures that the action feels as fluid as a real-time action game.

### The Mechanics of Crawling
In this game, movement is not just about repositioning; it is your primary method of interaction. The "Friendly Head Butt" is a core maneuver that can only be executed while crawling. This move allows players to displace smaller enemies without taking contact damage, creating gaps in the swarms that constantly close in.

Follow these steps to master the early game:
1. **Prioritize the Hammer:** The hammer is your most reliable starting tool for breaking environmental objects.
2. **Collect Floor Chicken:** Just like in the classics, floor chicken provides essential health regeneration.
3. **Use the Edible Vampire Repeller:** This 2021-era throwback item creates a temporary aura that keeps vampires at bay, allowing for safe navigation through the countryside.

| Mechanic | Function | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Turbo Turn** | Fast-paced turn-based movement | Keep moving to trigger auto-attacks |
| **Friendly Head Butt** | Knockback maneuver | Use to escape corners when surrounded |
| **Pawn Caller** | Upgrade Hub | Spend coins on "No Speechy" buffs |
| **Floor Chicken** | Healing | Save for when health drops below 30% |
