## Gameplay Mechanics: A New Way to Crawl

While the aesthetic might feel familiar to fans of the developer's previous work, the gameplay in Vampire Crawlers is a significant departure. The game is described as a "high-speed turn-based" crawler. This means that while you have the time to plan your moves, the intensity of the enemy waves requires quick thinking and a deep understanding of the turn-order queue.

The core loop involves navigating through procedurally generated maps based on the "ordinary Italian countryside." Players must manage resources, avoid traps, and utilize a variety of items to fend off the vampire hordes.

### Key Gameplay Features

1.  **Tactical Movement:** Every step counts. Moving into a tile occupied by an enemy triggers an automatic encounter, but positioning yourself diagonally can often provide a defensive bonus.
2.  **Resource Management:** You aren't just managing health; you are managing your "momentum," which dictates how many actions you can take per turn.
3.  **Environmental Interaction:** Use hammers to break walls or find hidden stashes of change to fund your mid-run upgrades.

| Mechanic | Impact on Gameplay | Strategy Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Turn-Order Queue** | Determines who attacks next | **Manipulate** the queue by using stun abilities. |
| **The Friendly Headbutt** | Low-cost physical knockback | Use this to **push** enemies into environmental traps. |
| **Momentum Meter** | Limits consecutive actions | **Save** momentum for boss phases to burst damage. |
