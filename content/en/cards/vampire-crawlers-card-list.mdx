## Mastering the Cascade Mechanic

The "Cascade" is the holy grail of Vampire Crawlers gameplay. It occurs when a card's effect triggers another card's activation criteria. For instance, a "Frost" card might have a 10% chance to trigger a "Shatter" card. If you have multiple "Shatter" cards in your deck, you can create a chain reaction.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful with "Infinite Cascades." While they look impressive, they can occasionally cause frame rate drops even on 2026 hardware if too many pixel-heavy effects overlap.

To optimize your cascades, look for these keywords in the **vampire crawlers card list**:
*   **Echo:** Repeats the last played card's effect.
*   **Chain:** Links the effect to the three nearest enemies.
*   **Resonance:** Increases damage for every card of the same color currently in your hand.
