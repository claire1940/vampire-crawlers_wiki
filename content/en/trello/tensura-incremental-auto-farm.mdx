## Setting Up Your First Tensura Incremental Auto Farm

To begin your journey toward automated greatness, you must reach the first milestone: unlocking the "Auto-Battle" toggle. This is usually achieved after defeating the Direwolf Boss in the initial forest zone. Once unlocked, the **tensura incremental auto farm** becomes your primary method of progression.

Follow these steps to initialize your setup:

1.  **Enable Auto-Skill Casting:** Navigate to the Great Sage menu and select the skills you want to cycle. Ensure you include at least one Area of Effect (AoE) skill to clear mobs faster.
2.  **Set Target Priority:** Focus on "Weakest First" during the early stages to ensure 100% kill uptime. As you grow stronger, switch to "Elite Focus" to maximize rare drops.
3.  **Configure Auto-Evolution:** If you have the "Predator" skill active, set it to automatically consume cores that match your current element to boost your base stats permanently.

The efficiency of your farm is heavily dictated by your "Kill Per Minute" (KPM) ratio. If you find that your character is taking more than three hits to kill a standard mob, you should move back to a previous zone. Efficiency is always better than struggling in a high-level area.

## Troubleshooting Efficiency Drops

Sometimes you might notice your resource gain slowing down. This is often referred to as "The Plateau." If your **tensura incremental auto farm** isn't performing as expected, consider the following factors:

*   **Inventory Overflow:** If your bag is full of common swords and armor, your Sage might stop looting Magicules. Enable "Auto-Sell" for anything below Rare tier.
*   **Skill Lag:** Using too many flashy AoE skills can actually slow down the server-side calculation of your kills. Stick to 2-3 high-efficiency skills.
*   **Movement Time:** If your character spends more than 2 seconds walking between mobs, your KPM will tank. Equip items with "Movement Speed" or use Souei's support buff.

By fine-tuning these small details, you ensure that every second spent away from the game is translated into maximum power for your Rimuru-inspired avatar.

## FAQ

**Q: Is using a third-party macro safe for a tensura incremental auto farm?**

A: While many players use simple auto-clickers, it is always safer to rely on the built-in "Great Sage" automation features. Using external scripts can lead to account flags or bans depending on the specific game's terms of service. Always prioritize in-game upgrades for automation.

**Q: What is the best way to increase Magicule gain per hour?**

A: The most effective method is to stack Shuna's support buff with the "Magicule Greed" passive skill. Additionally, ensure you are farming in a zone where you can one-shot the mobs to maintain the highest possible kill rate.

**Q: Does the auto farm work when the game is closed?**

A: This depends on whether the specific version of the game supports "Offline Gains." Most modern incremental games provide a percentage of your active farm rate (usually 50-75%) while offline. Investing in the "Offline Calculation" upgrade in the Great Sage tree can increase this percentage up to 100%.

**Q: When should I move to a new farming zone?**

A: You should move to a new zone when your current KPM (Kills Per Minute) exceeds 60 and you are no longer gaining significant level progress. If a new zone slows your KPM below 20, return to the previous area for more efficient grinding.
