The setting isn't just for show; the "Italian countryside magic" mentioned in the previews suggests that environmental hazards and secrets play a larger role than in previous titles. Exploring the map is just as important as killing monsters.

*   **Treasure Hunting**: Don't just follow the arrows. Hidden villas and vineyards often contain unique loot that can't be found in standard chests.
*   **Strategy vs. Speed**: You can choose to play "steadily" with a defensive build or go for "limit operations" (extreme speed) to clear the map as fast as possible. Both playstyles are viable and offer different ending rewards.
*   **Breaking the Combo**: Don't be afraid to use a skill that resets your combo if it means triggering a massive chain effect. Sometimes, resetting the board is better than maintaining a small multiplier.
