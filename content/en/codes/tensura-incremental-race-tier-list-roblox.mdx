## Evolution Mechanics and Skill Mastery

A common mistake new players make is attempting to evolve as soon as the button becomes available. To truly climb the **tensura incremental race tier list roblox** rankings, you must understand the relationship between skill mastery and evolution.

To successfully upgrade your skills and evolve into a more powerful form, you must follow a specific sequence. First, ensure your current skill mastery is at **100%**. Skipping this step or evolving prematurely can result in "bricked" builds where your skills do not scale correctly with your new racial multipliers.

### How to Evolve Correctly
1.  **Max Race Upgrades:** Before looking at evolution, ensure your current race's internal upgrades are maxed out using your accumulated Magicules.
2.  **Master Your Skills:** Use your skills repeatedly until the mastery bar hits 100%. This is the only way to ensure they upgrade to their "Enhanced" versions upon evolution.
3.  **Trigger Evolution:** Once mastery is full, initiate the evolution. This will reset your level but significantly increase your base power.

## The Role of Prestiges in Race Progression

In *Tensura Incremental*, your race choice is not permanent, but your prestige progress is. Prestiging allows you to earn "Souls" or "Essence," which can be spent on permanent talent trees that apply regardless of which race you choose in your next run.

Expert players often use "Fast-Prestige" cycles. Instead of trying to reach the final evolution every time, they evolve 2-3 times, hit a wall, and then prestige to gain multipliers. This makes the next climb through the **tensura incremental race tier list roblox** much faster.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not prestige until you have at least doubled your current total of Prestige Points. Doing so too early provides diminishing returns and can slow down your overall progress.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best starting race in Tensura Incremental?**

A: For most players, starting as a Slime is the best choice. While it starts weak, its evolution path into the True Demon Lord is currently the strongest overall path in the **tensura incremental race tier list roblox**.

**Q: Do I lose my race when I prestige?**

A: Yes, prestiging usually resets your current race to the base form, but you keep your permanent multipliers and unlocked "Race Souls," which make re-leveling much faster.

**Q: How do I get 100% skill mastery quickly?**

A: The fastest way to gain mastery is to fight enemies that are slightly higher level than you. Using skills against "Boss" type enemies also provides a significant bonus to mastery gain compared to standard mobs.

**Q: Can I change my race without prestiging?**

A: Changing your race mid-run usually requires a "Rebirth Gem" or a specific NPC interaction, but it is generally recommended to wait until you prestige to switch paths to maximize your efficiency.
