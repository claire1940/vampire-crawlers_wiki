## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my race in Tensura Incremental?**

A: You can change your race by using a "Rebirth Stone" or by reaching the end of your current evolution tree and selecting a "New Beginning" option. This usually resets your level but allows you to keep a portion of your mastered skill power.

**Q: What is the best starting race for a beginner?**

A: For most players, the **Human** or **Goblin** paths are the most straightforward. They have balanced stat gains and early access to powerful defensive skills. However, if you are looking at the **tensura incremental all races** meta for the long term, Slime offers the highest potential.

**Q: Why can't I evolve even though I have enough Souls?**

A: The most likely reason is that your Skill Mastery is not at 100%. Check your skill menu; if any active skill is below the maximum mastery level, the evolution button will remain locked or warn you of a "Sub-optimal Evolution."

**Q: Are there secret races not listed in the standard evolution tree?**

A: Yes, there are "Hidden Lineages" like the Phantom or the Insectar. These usually require specific environmental triggers, such as standing in a specific zone during a world event or having a specific combination of mastered skills from two different racial paths.
