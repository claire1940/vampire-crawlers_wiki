## Understanding the Value of Your Rewards

When you use **tensura incremental working codes**, you aren't just getting random currency; you are getting rolls that define your character's potential. Understanding how to spend these is key to efficient progression.

### Race Rolls
Races determine your base stats and your evolutionary path. While you start as a basic monster, a successful Race Roll can transform you into a High Elf, an Oni, or even a True Dragon. Each race has unique multipliers for Strength, Agility, and Magic.

### Skill Rolls
Skills are the active and passive abilities you use in combat. In this incremental experience, skills can range from basic elemental attacks to "Ultimate Skills" that can clear entire dungeon floors in seconds. Using codes to get free skill rolls allows you to bypass the early-game struggle of having low damage output.

### Spirit Rolls
Spirits act as companions or buffs that provide an "edge" in difficult boss fights. High-tier spirit rolls (like Spirit Roll 3) offer significantly better odds of landing a legendary spirit compared to standard rolls.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Do not use all your Race Rolls at once. If you land a "Rare" or "Epic" race early on, save the rest of your rolls for when you reach higher levels or when the meta shifts after a new update.
