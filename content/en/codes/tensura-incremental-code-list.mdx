## Understanding the Rewards: Race, Skill, and Spirit Rolls

If you are new to the game, you might wonder why the items in the **tensura incremental code list** are so highly valued. Each type of "roll" serves a specific purpose in your character's evolution.

### Race Rerolls
Your race determines your base stats and your potential evolutionary path. Starting as a weak monster, you can use Race Rerolls to try and land on a rare or powerful species. Some races have significantly higher health multipliers or unique passive traits that make surviving the early dungeon floors much easier.

### Skill Rolls
Skills are the active and passive abilities you use in combat. As you devour enemies, you gain power, but Skill Rolls allow you to bypass the grind and potentially unlock high-tier abilities early. These are crucial for defeating the bosses found on deeper dungeon floors.

### Spirit Rolls
Spirit Rolls are often categorized by tiers (like Spirit Roll 3). These provide enhancements to your spiritual pressure and overall combat effectiveness. In the late game, having a high-tier spirit can be the difference between clearing a raid and being sent back to the start of the floor.
