**Q: How do I evolve my race in Tensura Incremental?**

A: You must reach the required amount of Essence or Magicules and then visit the "Evolution Altar" in the main hub. Each race has different requirements, which can be viewed in the "Race" tab of your character menu.

**Q: Can I lose my race if I Rebirth?**
