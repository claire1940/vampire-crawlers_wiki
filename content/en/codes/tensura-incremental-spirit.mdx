## Getting Started with Tensura: Incremental

Your journey begins with basic physical training, but the real power lies in the spiritual path. Unlike traditional clicker games, Tensura: Incremental requires a strategic approach to stat distribution. Players must balance their Strength, Agility, and Intelligence to unlock specific spirit-based abilities.

The training process is "incremental," meaning your gains grow exponentially as you unlock better training areas and equipment. Early on, you should focus on completing the initial questline to gain access to the first set of skills. These skills range from Common to Unique, and finding the right combination is the key to efficient farming.
