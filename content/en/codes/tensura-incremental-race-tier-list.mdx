## Advanced Progression Tips

To truly master the **tensura incremental race tier list**, you need to look beyond just the raw numbers. The synergy between your race and your equipped items can often outweigh a simple tier ranking.

1.  **Synergize Skills:** If you are playing as a Kijin, focus on items that increase "Critical Strike" rather than "Magic Power."
2.  **Idle vs. Active:** If you prefer to leave the game running in the background, the **High Orc** or **Slime** paths are better due to their passive regeneration and defensive stats. If you are an active player, the **Demon** or **Kijin** paths offer more "click-based" power.
3.  **The "Wall" Strategy:** If you hit a progression wall where upgrades become too expensive, don't wait. Prestige immediately. It is always faster to prestige and regain your speed with a higher multiplier than to grind for hours for a single level.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best starting race in Tensura Incremental?**

A: For most players, starting as a **Slime** is the most efficient path. While it starts slow, the evolution into a Demon Slime and eventually a True Dragon offers the highest late-game multipliers on the current **tensura incremental race tier list**.

**Q: Do I need to max all skills before I can evolve my race?**

A: No. As discovered in recent 2026 gameplay guides, you only need to focus on maxing your **Race Upgrade**. Maxing every individual skill is not a requirement for evolution and can actually slow down your overall progression.

**Q: How often should I prestige?**

A: You should prestige as soon as your progress slows down significantly. In the early game, this might be every 30 minutes. In the late game, you might wait until you have reached a new evolution stage to maximize the Prestige Point payout.

**Q: Are there any secret races not on the tier list?**

A: There are "Hidden Evolutions" like the **Spirit-Wielder** which require specific items found in high-level dungeons. These are generally considered S-Tier but are much harder to obtain than the standard evolution paths.
