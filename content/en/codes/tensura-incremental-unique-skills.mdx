## The Foundation: Mastery and Race Evolution

Before you can even think about the flashy Ultimate Skills, you must first perfect your current arsenal. The progression system in Tensura Incremental is strictly linear; you cannot skip steps if you want to see meaningful stat growth. The first and most important rule of skill progression is reaching 100% mastery.

Mastery is gained through consistent use of your abilities in combat, whether you are grinding through dungeons or participating in raids. Once a skill reaches its maximum mastery level, it doesn't automatically transform. Instead, it becomes "ready" for the next stage of development.

| Progression Step | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Mastery** | Reach **100%** through usage | Unlocks the potential for evolution |
| **Race Evolution** | Meet level/soul requirements | Triggers the upgrade of mastered skills |
| **Unique Skill Maxing** | All unique slots at **100%** | Prerequisite for Ultimate Skill unlocking |
| **Ultimate Unlock** | **10,000 Souls** + Race Evolution | Grants access to "Lord" tier abilities |

> **Warning:** Never attempt a race evolution if your primary skills are below 100% mastery. Doing so may result in missed upgrade opportunities, forcing you to grind significantly more in your next evolutionary stage.

## Mastering Tensura Incremental Unique Skills

Every time you evolve your race, your unique skills undergo a transformation, becoming more efficient and dealing higher percentages of damage based on your Magicules and Existence Points (EP).

## Transitioning to Ultimate Skills

The latest 2026 updates have introduced a revamped UI and a more streamlined path toward Ultimate Skills. Once you have maxed out your **tensura incremental unique skills**, you enter the endgame phase. The transition to Ultimates is not just a matter of experience; it is a matter of resource management.

To unlock your first Ultimate, you must perform a specific second upgrade during your race evolution. This upgrade carries a steep price tag of **10,000 souls**. Once purchased, your unique skills will pave the way for "Lord" class abilities.

### How Ultimate Skills Function
Unlike unique or racial skills, Ultimate Skills cannot be spammed. They rely on a dedicated **Ultimate Meter** that appears in the new 2026 UI.

1.  **Charging the Meter:** You can only charge the meter by using **Basic Skills**. 
2.  **Restrictions:** Using racial skills, spirit skills, or **tensura incremental unique skills** will NOT contribute to your Ultimate Meter.
3.  **Magicule Cost:** Activating an Ultimate requires a massive reserve of Magicules, typically ranging from **2 million to 4 million**.

| Ultimate Skill | Primary Effect | Combat Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Powers** | Provides massive stat buffs | Support / Solo Buffer |
| **Disintegration** | High burst damage | Single Target DPS |
| **Lord of Purity** | Top-tier healing | Team Survival / Healer |
| **Lord of Chaos** | Damage + Buffs + Debuffs | Versatile All-rounder |
| **Lord of Void** | Strong damage + Debuffs | Crowd Control / DPS |
| **Lord of Wrath** | Highest raw damage in-game | Boss Killer |

> **Tip:** Lord of Wrath is currently the most powerful offensive tool in the game. In recent testing, it has been shown to delete nearly 50% of high-level boss HP (like Hinata) in a single activation.

## Farming Magicules and Existence Points (EP)

Since Ultimate Skills require millions of Magicules, your focus must shift toward increasing your maximum capacity. Magicules are directly correlated to your **Existence Points (EP)**. If your EP is low, you will find yourself unable to cast your most powerful abilities, even if your meter is full.

To farm EP efficiently in 2026, players should focus on two main activities:
*   **Training:** Consistent training sessions provide a steady, safe increase in EP.
*   **Raids:** High-level raids offer the fastest EP gains but require a coordinated team and a solid set of **tensura incremental unique skills** to survive.

## Advanced Skill Strategy

1.  **Opener:** Use your **tensura incremental unique skills** to debuff the enemy and buff yourself.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I upgrade my tensura incremental unique skills?**

A: To upgrade your skills, you must first reach 100% mastery with that specific skill. Once mastered, you must evolve your race. The act of evolving is what triggers the skill to upgrade to its next tier.

**Q: Why can't I use my Ultimate Skill even though the meter is full?**

A: Ultimate Skills require a massive amount of Magicules (usually between 2 and 4 million). If your Existence Points (EP) are too low, your Magicule capacity won't be high enough to trigger the skill. Focus on training or raids to increase your EP.

**Q: What is the best way to charge the Ultimate Meter?**

A: You must use Basic Skills to charge the meter. Racial skills, spirit skills, and unique skills do not contribute to the meter's progress.

**Q: Are unique skills still useful after unlocking Ultimates?**

A: Absolutely. While Ultimates deal the most damage, **tensura incremental unique skills** provide essential buffs and debuffs that make your Ultimates even more effective. They are also your primary source of utility while your Ultimate Meter is charging.
