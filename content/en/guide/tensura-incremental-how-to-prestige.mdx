While the game offers various paths, the "Human" path remains the most popular for those looking to cycle through prestiges quickly.

## Maximizing Race Upgrades

The most common point of confusion regarding **tensura incremental how to prestige** is the "Race Upgrade" bar. Unlike skills like "Sword Mastery" or "Magic Affinity," the Race Upgrade is a specific stat found in the evolution menu. 

You do not need to max out your skills to 100% to prestige. Many players spend days grinding skill levels only to realize the prestige button was waiting for them once their Race Upgrade hit the cap. Focus your resources here first. Once the bar is filled, the game recognizes your character has reached its current biological limit, allowing you to transcend through the prestige system.

## FAQ

**Q: Does prestiging reset my items and equipment?**

A: In the current version of Tensura Incremental, most standard items are reset upon prestige. However, "Legendary" or "Event" items often stay in your inventory, providing a significant head start for your next run. Always check the item description for the "Soulbound" tag.

**Q: How many souls do I actually need to prestige?**

A: While the requirement can vary based on your race, having at least 10,000 souls is the standard benchmark for a smooth **tensura incremental how to prestige** process. Some higher-tier races may require more, but for the Human speedrun strategy, 10,000 is the magic number.

**Q: Why is the prestige button not appearing even though I maxed my race?**

A: This is usually due to one of two things: either you haven't reached the minimum evolution stage required for that specific race, or you have a pending evolution that needs to be processed first. Ensure all evolution prompts are cleared and your Race Upgrade bar shows "MAX."

**Q: Can I prestige faster by playing solo?**

A: While solo play is possible, it is significantly slower. The fastest way to prestige is to join a public server and look for players running the "Abyssal Dungeon" or "Central City Raid." Tagging along for the rewards is the most efficient way to gather the necessary EP and Souls.
