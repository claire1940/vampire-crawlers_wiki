## Understanding the Evolution Mechanic

In Tensura Incremental, skills do not evolve simply by using them repeatedly. While "Skill Mastery" is a prerequisite, the actual evolution event is hard-coded to your character's biological progression. This mimics the anime's lore, where Rimuru and his subordinates only gained "Gift" or "Evolution" bonuses upon reaching a new racial tier (such as evolving from a Slime to a Demon Slime).

The game operates on a dual-requirement system. You cannot evolve a skill if your race is too low, and you cannot benefit from a race evolution if your skill mastery hasn't reached the maximum threshold. This synergy is what often confuses players who focus solely on one aspect while neglecting the other.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to prestige or perform a major race evolution until you have checked your current skill mastery percentages. Evolving your race without 100% mastery on your core skills may result in missed opportunities for immediate power spikes.

## Requirements for Skill Evolution

Before you can trigger an evolution, you must satisfy specific conditions within the game's UI. Unlike other incremental games where upgrades are purchased with currency, skill evolution is an automated reward for reaching specific milestones.

### 1. Skill Mastery (100%)
Every skill in your inventory has a mastery percentage. This increases as you use the skill in combat or during training sessions in specific zones. You must ensure that the skill you wish to evolve has reached the **100% Mastery** mark. If a skill is at 99.9%, the race evolution will not trigger the upgrade for that specific ability.

### 2. Race Evolution
This is the "trigger" event. In Tensura Incremental, your race is your primary level of progression. As you upgrade your race through the evolution menu (usually requiring materials from dungeons or specific boss drops), the game checks your mastered skills. Upon a successful race evolution, any skill at 100% mastery will automatically transform into its next tier.

## Efficient Mastery Farming Techniques

Since 100% mastery is the primary bottleneck for **tensura incremental how to evolve skill**, optimizing your farm is key. Don't just mindlessly click; use the environment to your advantage.

1.  **Training Dummies:** If available in the current hub, use training dummies. They have infinite health, allowing you to cycle through your skill rotations without waiting for mob respawns.
2.  **Health Regeneration Builds:** Equip items that boost your health regen and stand in a pack of low-damage mobs. Set your skills to auto-cast (if you have the gamepass or script) to farm mastery while away from your keyboard.
3.  **Multi-Target Skills:** Mastery is calculated per hit. Using Area of Effect (AoE) skills on large groups of enemies will fill your mastery bar significantly faster than single-target attacks.

## Troubleshooting Common Evolution Issues

If you have followed the steps for **tensura incremental how to evolve skill** but your abilities remain unchanged, consider these common bugs and fixes:

*   **The 99% Bug:** Sometimes the UI rounds up. If your skill says 100% but hasn't evolved, use it a few more times to ensure it has actually reached the internal cap.
*   **Race Path Conflicts:** Some races have branching evolution paths. Ensure the path you chose supports the skill you are trying to evolve. For example, a "Physical" focused evolution path may not trigger evolutions for "Magic" based skills.
*   **Server Lag:** In high-population servers, the "check" for skill evolution during a race upgrade can lag. It is often safer to evolve your race in a private server to ensure the script triggers correctly.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to max out every single skill to evolve my race?**

A: No. You only need to max the mastery for the specific skills you want to evolve. However, to progress your race, you only need to meet the material and level requirements. You can evolve your race with 0% mastery on your skills, but those skills will remain in their basic form until your *next* race evolution.

**Q: What happens if I evolve my race but my skill mastery was only at 80%?**

A: The skill will not evolve. You will have to wait until your next racial evolution milestone to attempt the skill evolution again. This is why reaching 100% mastery before clicking the "Evolve" button is the most critical part of **tensura incremental how to evolve skill**.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many times a skill can evolve?**

A: Most skills have 3 to 4 evolution stages, ending in an "Ultimate" or "God" tier. These final stages usually require the "True Demon Lord" or "Hero" race tiers.

**Q: Can I reset my skills if I evolved the wrong ones?**

A: Currently, the game allows for skill resets through "Prestige" mechanics or specific rare items found in high-level dungeons. Resetting usually refunds a portion of your materials but will reset your mastery to zero.
