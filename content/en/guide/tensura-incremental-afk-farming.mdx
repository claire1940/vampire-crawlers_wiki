## Core Mechanics of AFK Progression

Before diving into specific locations, you must understand how the incremental systems interact. Your primary currency is Magicules, which are generated by defeating enemies and completing passive quests. As you accumulate these, you can invest them into your "Great Sage" interface to automate various functions.

The game relies heavily on a prestige system. Each time you "Reincarnate," you lose your current level and Magicules but gain a permanent percentage boost to all future gains. For effective **tensura incremental afk farming**, you should aim to reach a level where your kill speed slows down significantly before hitting the reset button.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I stop my character from disconnecting during tensura incremental afk farming?**

A: Ensure you are using a stable internet connection and have disabled your computer's sleep mode. In-game, most players use a simple auto-clicker set to click every 60 seconds to prevent the Roblox idle-kick timer from triggering.

**Q: What is the best evolution for pure AFK speed?**

A: The "True Demon Lord" evolution is currently the most balanced for AFK. It provides a massive boost to AoE damage and unlocks the "Harvest Festival" passive, which doubles Magicule gains from mobs for the first 4 hours of a session.

**Q: Should I focus on Magicules or Souls first?**

A: Magicules are better for early-game progression and upgrading your base stats. However, once you hit level 500, Souls become the bottleneck for evolution. At that point, you should switch your **tensura incremental afk farming** focus to the Orc Disaster Plains, which has the highest Soul drop rate.

**Q: Can I farm rolls for skills while AFK?**

A: Yes, by setting your character to farm "Boss Instances," you can accumulate Boss Tokens. These tokens can be traded in the shop for Skill Reset Scrolls and Rare Skill Rolls. Make sure your "Auto-Restart" is enabled for the specific instance you are farming.
