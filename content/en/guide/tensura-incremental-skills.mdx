## How to Upgrade and Evolve Skills

Upgrading your abilities in Tensura Incremental is not as simple as clicking a "level up" button. The process is intrinsically tied to your character's biological progression. You cannot simply brute-force your way to higher tiers without first proving your proficiency with your current kit.

The primary bottleneck most players encounter is the Mastery requirement. Before any skill can be upgraded or evolved into a higher form, you must reach **100% Mastery** with that specific skill. This is achieved through consistent use in combat and training. Once your mastery is capped, the actual "upgrade" occurs when you perform a **Race Evolution**.

| Step | Action | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Mastery Grinding** | Use skills in battle repeatedly | Reaches **100% Mastery** |
| **2** | **Soul Collection** | Defeat enemies/bosses | Accumulate necessary **Souls** |
| **3** | **Race Evolution** | Evolution Menu | Upgrades mastered skills to next tier |
| **4** | **Ultimate Unlock** | Second Evolution + 10,000 Souls | Unlocks **Ultimate Skill** slot |

> **Warning:** Attempting to evolve your race before hitting 100% mastery on your core skills may result in missed upgrade opportunities. Always verify your mastery tab before spending your hard-earned souls.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills

The 2026 updates have introduced a revamped UI and a much more structured path to obtaining Ultimate Skills. These are the pinnacle of **tensura incremental skills**, capable of turning the tide of a raid in seconds. However, the requirements are steep and require significant investment in both time and resources.

To unlock your first Ultimate, you must have your Unique Skills at maximum mastery. Following this, you must progress through your racial evolution tree. The specific trigger for Ultimates is typically the **second major evolution** after reaching the unique tier, which carries a hefty price tag of **10,000 Souls**.

### The Ultimate Meter and Magicules
Using an Ultimate Skill isn't just about waiting for a cooldown. It requires two specific resources:
1.  **Ultimate Meter:** This bar must be filled to 100% before an Ultimate can be triggered.
2.  **Magicules:** Ultimates consume a massive amount of Magicules, usually ranging between **2 million and 4 million** per cast.

To charge your Ultimate Meter efficiently, you must use **Basic Skills**. It is important to note that Racial Skills, Spirit Skills, and Unique Skills do **not** contribute to the meter charge. Only pure basics will build the energy needed for your finisher.

## Increasing Magicules and Existence Points

Since Ultimate Skills require millions of Magicules, increasing your capacity is vital. Your maximum Magicule count is tied directly to your **Existence Points (EP)**. To increase your EP, you must engage in the game's core loop of training and raiding.

*   **Training:** Best for consistent, AFK-friendly EP gains.
*   **Raids:** The fastest way to gain large chunks of EP and Souls simultaneously.
*   **Existence Points:** These act as your "power level" indicator and determine the ceiling of your magical energy.

## Advanced Combat Strategy

To maximize the efficiency of your **tensura incremental skills**, you should adopt a "rotation" mindset. Because only basic skills charge your ultimate meter, your combat flow should look like this:

1.  **Opener:** Use Unique or Racial skills to apply initial debuffs or buffs.
2.  **Charging Phase:** Spam your Basic Skills to build the Ultimate Meter as quickly as possible.
3.  **Execution:** Once the meter is full and you have at least 3 million Magicules, trigger your Ultimate (e.g., Lord of Wrath).
4.  **Recovery:** Use support skills or Lord of Purity to heal while your Magicules regenerate through EP-based passive recovery.

## FAQ

**Q: Why aren't my tensura incremental skills upgrading when I level up?**

A: Skills do not upgrade based on your character level. You must reach 100% Mastery with the skill first and then perform a Race Evolution to trigger the upgrade to the next tier.

**Q: How do I charge the Ultimate Meter faster?**

A: The Ultimate Meter only charges when you use Basic Skills. Avoid using Unique, Racial, or Spirit skills if your primary goal is to quickly fill the meter for an Ultimate attack.

**Q: What is the best Ultimate Skill for solo players?**

A: Lord of Wrath is currently considered the best for solo play due to its massive damage output, which allows you to clear bosses like Hinata much faster. However, Lord of Chaos is a close second for its balance of buffs and debuffs.

**Q: How much do Ultimate Skills cost to unlock?**

A: Beyond the prerequisite of maxing your unique skills and evolving your race, the specific unlock for the Ultimate slot typically costs 10,000 Souls during your second major evolution phase.
