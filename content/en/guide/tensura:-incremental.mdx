## Understanding the Race System

Your race determines your base stat multipliers and potential for growth. While most players start with common or uncommon races, the ultimate goal is to roll for legendary variants that provide massive combat bonuses.

The April 2026 update introduced the **True Dragon** race, which has shifted the meta significantly. It is currently considered the strongest race in the game due to its "insane" bonus stats upon upgrading. However, obtaining it requires immense luck or persistence during special event weeks.

| Race Type | Rarity / Drop Chance | Primary Benefit |

## Efficient Training and Stat Progression

In **Tensura: Incremental**, your character's power is divided into several key attributes. To progress through the letter grades (from F to A and beyond), you must balance your training time between physical conditioning and spiritual meditation.

### Early Game Training Priorities
When starting at Grade F or E, focus on basic conditioning to build a foundation. As you unlock more advanced training methods, shift your focus to maximize your Energy Projection (EP) and Experience (XP) gains.

| Training Method | XP Reward | EP Reward | Recommended Grade |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Conditioning** | Low | Low | Grade F - E |
| **Combat Drills** | 100 | 250 | Grade E - D |
| **Spiritual Meditation** | 225 | 500 | Grade D+ |
| **Aura Stabilization** | High | Medium | Grade C+ |

### Stat Allocation Strategy
Using the Analyst tool, you can spend your earned points to upgrade specific attributes. For a balanced build that can handle most missions, follow this priority:

1.  **Melee Damage:** Essential for finishing off enemies when mana is low.
2.  **Magical Regeneration:** Critically important for using high-tier skills like Black Flame.
3.  **Health Regen:** Prevents you from being "one-tapped" by higher-grade bosses.
4.  **Skill Damage:** Scales your unique abilities for massive burst damage.

## Combat and Mission Mastery

Missions are the primary way to test your strength and earn rare rewards. Combat is turn-based, requiring careful management of your "Magicals" (mana). If your magical reserves are low, using the **Skip Turn** mechanic is a valid strategy to regenerate resources before unleashing a powerful attack.

### Essential Skills for 2026
Equipping the right skills can make Grade A missions significantly easier. You should aim to have a mix of offensive spells and utility buffs.

*   **Steal Strength:** A high-damage move that can often one-tap lower-level enemies.
*   **Black Lightning/Black Flame:** Powerful elemental attacks for clearing high-health bosses.
*   **Body Armor Magic:** Provides a defensive layer, though it should be used sparingly as it consumes magical energy that might be better spent on offense.
*   **Dirt Kick:** A useful utility skill for disrupting enemy turns.

> **Tip:** If you find yourself losing more than 50% of your health in a single turn, flee the battle immediately. There is no shame in retreating to train your Health and Aura Stabilization further.

## Advanced Progression: The Path to Grade A

Reaching Grade A in **Tensura: Incremental** marks the transition into the mid-to-late game. At this stage, you should be farming Flame Giants and Hobgoblin Soldiers with ease. 

1.  **Automate Training:** Use the highest tier of meditation available to you.
2.  **Upgrade Magical Generation:** Ensure you can use at least two high-tier skills back-to-back without running dry.
3.  **Equip Unique Skills:** Check your skill inventory for "Unique" or "Ultimate" tier abilities that offer better scaling than basic melee.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the True Dragon race in Tensura: Incremental?**

A: The True Dragon race is a super rare drop with a 0.25% chance when rolling your race. The best strategy is to wait for a "2x Week" or "Double Bonus" event to increase your chances and maximize your rolls.

**Q: What should I do if I keep getting defeated in missions?**

A: If you are struggling, focus on training your Health and Magical Regeneration stats. Most players fail because they lack the "Magicals" to sustain their skills. Spend more time in Spiritual Meditation to boost your EP and upgrade your defense via the Analyst.

**Q: Are there active codes for Tensura: Incremental in 2026?**

A: Yes, the developers frequently release new codes alongside major updates, such as the True Dragon update. Check the official game description or community Discord for the most recent codes to get free rolls and XP boosts.

**Q: Which training method is the best for fast leveling?**

A: Once you reach Grade D, **Spiritual Meditation** is generally the most efficient method as it provides 500 EP and 225 XP per cycle. For lower levels, stick to Combat Drills until your stats are high enough to handle meditation.
