## Understanding Reaper Scaling Mechanics

Before attempting the run, you must understand why traditional high-level builds often fail against the Reaper. In Vampire Crawlers, the Reaper’s health is not a static value. Instead, it scales exponentially based on your character's level at the moment it spawns.

For every level your character gains, the Reaper receives an additional **655,000 HP**. If you reach the 30-minute mark at level 100, the Reaper will have over 65 million health, making it nearly impossible to kill before more Reapers spawn and overwhelm you. However, by staying at level 2, the Reaper spawns with approximately **1.22 million HP**, a manageable number if you have the correct weapon setup.

## Essential Power-Up Configuration

To successfully execute this **vampire crawlers trophy guide** strategy, you must first reset your power-ups. This does not cost you any gold; you can refund and reinvest at any time. The goal is to maximize your survivability and damage while strictly avoiding any power-ups that force unwanted leveling.

### What to Upgrade
Focus on stats that improve your weapon's efficiency without increasing your XP intake.
*   **Might:** Increases damage output.
*   **Cooldown:** Allows weapons like the Clock Lancet to fire faster.
*   **Area:** Increases the size of your protection zone.
*   **Speed & Duration:** Enhances weapon travel and uptime.
*   **Amount:** Crucial for creating more projectiles.

### What to Avoid
> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not upgrade **Magnet, Growth, or Curse**. Magnet and Growth will cause you to accidentally pick up XP gems from a distance, leveling you up prematurely. Curse increases enemy health and speed, making the Reaper significantly harder to kill.

## The Library Strategy: Step-by-Step

This method requires using the character **Clerici**. We choose Clerici because of their unique temporary area boost. At the start of a run, Clerici has a massive AOE (Area of Effect) bonus that slowly diminishes as they level up. By staying at level 2, we "freeze" this massive area boost, allowing our weapons to cover the entire screen and protect us from all incoming damage until the 30-minute mark.

### Initial Setup
1.  Select the **Library** stage.
2.  Enable **Frantic Mode** (to ensure enough XP is available at the very end).
3.  Immediately move to the top of the map and grab the **Green Gem**.
4.  You must obtain the **Runetracer** from this level-up. If you do not see Runetracer, reset the run immediately.

### Perfect Positioning
Once you have the Runetracer, you must wedge yourself into a specific spot in the Library environment. Look for a clock pendulum on the wall and a small table. 
*   Align Clerici’s hand with the clock's pendulum.
*   Align Clerici’s belt buckle with the vertical line on the wall near the table.

If positioned correctly, the Runetracer will bounce in a tight pattern directly on top of your character, creating an impenetrable wall of damage.

## Mid-Game: The Red Gem Mechanic

While you are standing still, you will notice that enemies die, but few gems appear on the ground. This is an optimization feature of the game engine. To maintain a high frame rate, the game stops rendering individual XP gems after a certain limit and instead condenses all that "missing" experience into a single **Red Gem**.

> **💡 Tip:** Keep an eye on where the Red Gem spawns. It usually appears just off-screen to the right or left. You will need to grab this exactly at the 30-minute mark to instantly jump from Level 2 to Level 100+.

## Final Build for the Reaper Kill

When the clock hits 30:00 and the Reaper spawns, immediately run to the Red Gem. This will trigger a massive leveling sequence. You must choose specific weapons and passives to ensure the Reaper stays frozen and dies quickly.

| Slot Type | Recommended Items | Reasoning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Weapon** | **Clock Lancet** | Freezes the Reaper in place indefinitely. |
| **Core Weapon** | **Laurel** | Provides a shield in case the Reaper touches you. |
| **Core Weapon** | **Runetracer** | Main source of bouncing DPS. |
| **Core Passive** | **Empty Tome** | Maximizes cooldown reduction for the Lancet. |
| **Core Passive** | **Spellbinder** | Increases the duration of the freeze effect. |
| **Core Passive** | **Candelabrador** | Maintains the large area of your weapons. |

**Avoid these items:** Lightning Ring, Fire Wand, and Axe. These weapons have random targeting and may hit secondary Reapers that spawn later, rather than focusing all damage on the primary Reaper you need to kill for the trophy.

## Executing the Kill

After leveling up to 100+ using the Red Gem, move to the final waiting spot just to the right of the starting zone. You are looking for a small table and a specific floor tile line. 
1.  Position yourself so the **Clock Lancet** is consistently hitting the Reaper.
2.  Ensure your **Runetracers** are trapped in the geometry of the wall/table to maximize hits per second.
3.  Stay still. If your cooldown is high enough, the Reaper will be permanently frozen. 

It may take a few minutes of sustained fire, but eventually, the Reaper will fall, unlocking the "Mask of the Red Death" and completing one of the hardest segments of any **vampire crawlers trophy guide**. ## FAQ

**Q: Why do I need to stay at Level 2 for the vampire crawlers trophy guide strategy?**

A: The Reaper's health is calculated as (655,000 x Your Level). By staying at level 2, you ensure the Reaper has the lowest possible health (approx. 1.2 million), making it killable with standard weapons.

**Q: Can I use any character other than Clerici?**

A: While possible, Clerici is highly recommended because her temporary Area bonus is massive at level 2. This allows your Runetracer and other weapons to protect you from all enemies without you needing to move or aim.

**Q: What happens if I accidentally pick up a Magnet?**

A: If you pick up a Magnet or the Attractorb, you will likely start leveling up from gems on the floor. This will increase the Reaper's health and likely ruin the "Level 2" strategy. It is best to restart the run if this happens.

**Q: Do I need to kill all the Reapers that spawn?**

A: No. You only need to defeat the very first Reaper that spawns at the 30-minute mark to unlock the Red Death character and the associated trophy. Once the first one dies, the achievement should pop immediately.
