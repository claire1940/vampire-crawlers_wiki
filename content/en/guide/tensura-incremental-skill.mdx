## The Foundation of Skill Mastery

Before you can think about the flashy Ultimate Skills that dominate the late-game meta, you must first master the basics. Every skill in the game begins with a mastery percentage. This percentage represents your character's proficiency with that specific ability. In the current 2026 build of the game, neglecting mastery is the most common mistake new players make.

To upgrade a skill, you must reach **100% mastery**. This is a hard requirement that cannot be bypassed by spending currency or using items. Mastery is typically gained through repeated use of the skill in combat or through specific training exercises found in the training grounds.

### Skill Progression Steps

| Step | Action | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Skill Usage | Active Combat/Training | Increases Mastery % |
| **2** | Max Mastery | Reach **100%** | Prepares skill for evolution |
| **3** | Race Evolution | Meet Level/Soul requirements | Upgrades skill to the next tier |
| **4** | Soul Infusion | 10,000 Souls (for Ultimates) | Unlocks Ultimate Skill tier |

> **Tip:** Do not attempt to evolve your race before your primary skills are at 100% mastery. Doing so may result in missed upgrade opportunities, forcing you to grind mastery at a higher level where enemies are significantly more dangerous.

## Evolving Your Race to Upgrade Skills

In Tensura Incremental, skill upgrades are intrinsically tied to your racial evolution. Unlike other RPGs where skills might level up automatically as you use them, here, the evolution of your physical and spiritual form is what triggers the skill's transformation. 

When you trigger a racial evolution, the game checks your current mastery levels. If a skill has reached its maximum potential, the evolution process will "awaken" that skill, turning a Basic skill into a Unique skill, or a Unique skill into an Ultimate skill. This system mirrors the progression seen in the original "That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime" series, providing a lore-accurate experience for fans.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills: The Path to Power

The 2026 updates have introduced a revamped UI and a more streamlined path to obtaining Ultimate Skills. These are the pinnacle of the **tensura incremental skill** tree, offering world-shaking power at a high cost.

To unlock an Ultimate Skill, you must follow a specific sequence:
1. Ensure your **Unique Skills** are at 100% mastery.
2. Initiate a high-tier racial evolution.
3. Purchase the **second upgrade** during the evolution process, which currently costs **10,000 Souls**.

Once these conditions are met, your Unique skills will be replaced by their "Lord" counterparts. These skills do not just do more damage; they often add entirely new mechanics to your kit, such as debuffs, massive healing, or area-of-effect (AoE) destruction.

### Ultimate Skill Mechanics and Magicules

Using an Ultimate Skill is not as simple as pressing a button. It requires two distinct resources:
*   **The Ultimate Meter:** This bar must be filled to 100% before an Ultimate can be cast.
*   **Magicules:** A massive amount of magical energy, typically ranging from **2 million to 4 million magicules** per cast.

To charge your Ultimate Meter, you must use **Basic Skills** exclusively. Using Racial skills, Spirit skills, or Unique skills will not contribute to the meter. This creates a tactical combat loop where you must cycle through your weaker abilities to "build up" to your finishing move.

> **Warning:** Ultimate skills are incredibly expensive. Ensure you have farmed enough **Existence Points (EP)** through training or raids to boost your Magicule capacity, or you may find yourself unable to cast your most powerful abilities when they are needed most.

## Farming Existence Points and Magicules

Since Ultimate Skills require millions of magicules, increasing your Magicule cap is a top priority in the mid-to-late game. This is handled through the **Existence Points (EP)** system. EP acts as a general measure of your character's total power and directly influences your resource pools.

The most efficient ways to farm EP in 2026 include:
*   **Raid Bosses:** Engaging in high-tier raids like the Hinata encounter provides massive EP rewards.
*   **AFK Training:** For players who cannot be active 24/7, the training grounds offer a steady, albeit slower, progression of EP.
*   **Quest Completion:** Main story quests often provide "milestone" EP boosts that are necessary for reaching the next racial evolution tier.

## Changes to the Health System in 2026

It is important to note that recent updates have significantly altered the health system in Tensura Incremental. Compared to previous versions, increasing your HP is now much more expensive. Many players have reported having roughly half the health they used to have at similar levels. This change makes defensive **tensura incremental skill** choices, like **Lord of Purity**, much more valuable than they were in the past. Survival now depends on active healing and clever use of buffs rather than just having a massive health pool to soak up damage.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I increase my tensura incremental skill mastery quickly?**

A: The fastest way to increase mastery is by using skills against enemies that are close to your own level. Using skills in the training zone is safer but yields mastery at a slower rate than active combat in dungeons or raids.

**Q: Why can't I use my Ultimate Skill even though my meter is full?**

A: You likely lack the required Magicules. Most Ultimate Skills require between 2 and 4 million Magicules. Check your current Magicule count and increase your EP through training to raise your maximum capacity.

**Q: Can I have multiple Ultimate Skills at once?**

A: Yes, as you evolve your race further and invest more Souls (10,000 per upgrade), you can unlock and equip multiple Ultimate Skills, allowing for complex skill rotations in battle.

**Q: What is the best way to charge the Ultimate Meter?**

A: You must use **Basic Skills** to charge the meter. Avoid using Unique or Racial skills if your primary goal is to build up your Ultimate Meter quickly, as they do not contribute to the charge.
