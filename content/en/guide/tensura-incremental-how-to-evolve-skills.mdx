## The Foundation: Reaching 100% Skill Mastery

Before you can even consider evolving a skill into its more powerful counterpart, you must achieve full mastery. In the 2026 update, the UI has been overhauled to make tracking this progress much easier, but the core requirement remains the same. Every skill, whether it is a basic attack or a unique ability, has a mastery percentage that increases as you use it in battle or through specific training exercises.

You cannot skip this step. If your mastery is sitting at 99%, the evolution triggers will simply not activate. Many players find themselves stuck because they attempt to evolve their race or spend souls before their current kit is fully "learned."

| Requirement | Description | Impact on Evolution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **100% Mastery** | Maximum proficiency reached through repeated use. | Mandatory prerequisite for all skill upgrades. |
| **Unique Skills** | High-tier abilities specific to your build. | Must be maxed to unlock the "Ultimate" tier. |
| **Basic Skills** | Standard attacks used in early game. | Essential for charging the Ultimate Meter later on. |

> **Warning:** Attempting a racial evolution without hitting 100% mastery on your primary skills will result in a successful race change but will fail to upgrade your abilities, essentially wasting the evolution opportunity.

## Racial Evolution: The Catalyst for Skill Upgrades

In Tensura Incremental, your skills are tied directly to your biological form. To upgrade your standard skills into their advanced versions, you must trigger a Racial Evolution. This is the primary answer to **tensura incremental how to evolve skills**: your skills don't just "level up" on their own; they transform when your character transcends their current race.

Once you have secured 100% mastery, navigate to the evolution menu. By evolving your race, the game automatically checks your mastery levels and "awakens" the associated skills. This process is relatively straightforward for early-game transitions, but it becomes significantly more expensive and complex as you move toward the Demon Lord or True Dragon tiers.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills

The pinnacle of the **tensura incremental how to evolve skills** journey is the acquisition of Ultimate Skills. These are game-changing abilities that can wipe out entire raid bosses or provide massive buffs to your party. However, the path to unlocking them is gated behind a specific "Second Upgrade" system introduced in the recent UI revamp.

To unlock your Ultimates, follow these specific steps:
1. Ensure all your Unique Skills have reached **100% Mastery**.
2. Initiate your Racial Evolution.
3. Purchase the **Second Upgrade**, which currently costs **10,000 Souls**.

This second upgrade is the specific trigger that transforms your maxed Unique Skills into Ultimate Skills. Without that 10,000 soul investment, you will remain at the Unique tier regardless of your race.

| Ultimate Skill | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |

| **Lord of Void** | High Damage + Debuffs | Crowd control and AOE clearing |

## Managing Magicules and Existence Points (EP)

Evolution is not just about having the right skills; it is about having the "capacity" to hold them. Ultimate Skills are incredibly taxing on your character's energy reserves. Most Ultimates require between **2 million and 4 million Magicules** to activate. 

If you find that your skills are evolved but you cannot cast them, you likely lack the necessary Magicule capacity. To increase this, you must farm **Existence Points (EP)**. EP acts as a permanent stat booster that expands your magicule pool and overall power level.

**Ways to Farm EP in 2026:**
*   **Training Grounds:** Consistent, low-risk EP gain for AFK players.
*   **Raids:** High-reward combat encounters that provide massive EP chunks upon completion.
*   **Dungeons:** Good for balancing gear drops with EP progression.

> **Tip:** Focus on Existence Points early. It is better to have a large magicule pool and basic skills than to have an Ultimate Skill you can only fire once every ten minutes.

## How to Use Evolved Skills in Combat

Once you have mastered **tensura incremental how to evolve skills**, you need to understand the new combat flow. Ultimate Skills cannot be spammed. They rely on an "Ultimate Meter" that must be filled before the skill becomes available.

Interestingly, the game rewards the use of your most basic abilities. To charge the Ultimate Meter, you must use **Basic Skills**. Using Racial Skills, Spirit Skills, or Unique Skills will not contribute to the meter. This creates a strategic loop where you must cycle through your basic rotations to "earn" the right to use your Lord-tier abilities.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I see the option for tensura incremental how to evolve skills in my menu?**

A: The evolution options are context-sensitive. If you haven't reached 100% mastery on your current skills or if you haven't reached the level cap for your current race, the evolution button may be hidden or greyed out. Double-check your mastery tab first.

**Q: Do I lose my old skills when I evolve?**

A: No, you do not lose them. Instead, they are upgraded into more powerful versions. For example, a Unique skill will transform into an Ultimate skill if you have met the soul and mastery requirements during your racial evolution.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get 10,000 souls for the Ultimate upgrade?**

A: The most efficient way to farm souls in 2026 is by participating in high-level Raids or farming the latest Dungeon bosses. Using certain area-of-effect (AOE) basic skills can also speed up the process when farming weaker mobs in bulk.

**Q: Is Lord of Wrath better than Lord of Void?**

A: It depends on your goal. Lord of Wrath currently holds the record for the highest single-target burst damage, making it ideal for bossing. Lord of Void offers better utility and debuffs, which is often preferred for clearing difficult waves of enemies or in PvP scenarios.
