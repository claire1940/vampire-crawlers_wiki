## Unlocking Ultimate Skills

Ultimate skills represent the pinnacle of your character's power. Unlike basic or unique skills, these require a specific sequence of upgrades and a significant investment in souls. To even begin the process of unlocking an ultimate, you must first reach 100% mastery with your unique skills.

### The Evolution Path

Once your unique skills are mastered, you must focus on your racial evolution. The second major upgrade in the evolution tree is the most critical; it costs **10,000 souls** and serves as the gateway to the ultimate skill menu. Without this specific upgrade, your character will remain capped at unique-tier power levels regardless of your level or EP.

1.  **Master Unique Skills:** Reach 100% mastery through constant usage in battle.
2.  **Collect Souls:** Farm 10,000 souls through prestige or high-level boss raids.
3.  **Evolve Race:** Purchase the second racial evolution tier.
4.  **Charge the Meter:** Use basic skills in combat to fill the Ultimate Meter.

## Ultimate Skills Comparison

Once you have unlocked the ability to use ultimates, you will need to choose which "Lord" skill to prioritize. Each ultimate skill consumes a massive amount of magicules—usually between **2 million and 4 million**—making them high-cost, high-reward assets.

| Ultimate Skill | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Wrath** | Extreme Burst Damage | Boss Finishing / Raids |
| **Lord of Purity** | High-Efficiency Healing | Survival in High-Tier Raids |
| **Lord of Chaos** | Damage Buff + Enemy Debuff | General Farming & DPS |
| **Lord of Void** | AOE Damage + Debuffs | Clearing Crowds of Mobs |
| **Lord of Powers** | Strong Stat Buffs | Team Support |

While **Lord of Wrath** currently holds the record for the highest single-hit damage—capable of taking nearly 50% of Hinata's HP in a single strike—it is important to note that you cannot charge your ultimate meter using racial or spirit skills. You must rely entirely on **basic skills** to build up the energy required for these attacks.

## Farming Magicules and Existence Points (EP)

To support the heavy costs of a high-tier **tensura incremental race** build, you must optimize your Magicule and EP farming. Magicules act as your mana pool, and their maximum capacity is directly tied to your Existence Points.

### Existence Points (EP) Sources
*   **Training:** The most consistent way to gain EP during AFK periods.
*   **Raids:** High-level raids provide the largest chunks of EP but require a competent build to complete.
*   **Evolution Bonuses:** Each time you evolve your race, your EP gain multiplier increases.

> **Warning:** The 2026 health system update has made HP significantly more expensive to upgrade. Players often find themselves with half the health they had in previous versions for the same soul cost. Prioritize defensive ultimate skills like **Lord of Purity** if you find yourself dying too quickly in raids.

## Strategic Progression for 2026

For players looking to maximize their efficiency, the progression loop in Tensura Incremental revolves around the prestige system. When you prestige, you lose your current level but gain points that can be used to roll for a better **tensura incremental race**. 

If you are currently a lower-tier race like a Goblin or Slime, your priority should be reaching the prestige threshold as quickly as possible. Do not invest heavily in mastering skills that you plan to replace. Once you land an A-tier race or higher, begin the grind for the 10,000 souls needed for Ultimate Skills.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best tensura incremental race for F2P players?**

A: While True Dragon is the best overall, the **Demon Peer** or **Kijin** races are excellent for F2P players. They offer high enough stat multipliers to clear all current 2026 content without requiring the extreme luck of a 0.25% drop rate.

**Q: How do I charge my Ultimate Meter quickly?**

A: You must use **Basic Skills** only. Racial, Spirit, and Unique skills do not contribute to the Ultimate Meter. Spamming low-cooldown basic attacks is the fastest way to prepare your "Lord" skills.

**Q: Why did my health decrease after the latest update?**

A: The developers revamped the health scaling system in 2026. Health is now more expensive to purchase with souls, resulting in lower total HP pools for most players. This was done to emphasize the importance of healing skills and defensive buffs.

**Q: How many magicules do I need for Ultimate Skills?**

A: Most Ultimate Skills require between **2,000,000 and 4,000,000 magicules** per cast. You can increase your maximum magicule capacity by increasing your Existence Points (EP) through training and successful raid completions.
