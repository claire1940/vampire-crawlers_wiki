### How to Use and Charge Ultimate Skills
Ultimate Skills do not operate like your standard attacks. They require a dedicated **Ultimate Meter** to be filled before they can be activated. 

*   **Charging the Meter:** You can only charge your ultimate by using **Basic Skills**. 
*   **Restrictions:** Using Racial Skills, Spirit Skills, or Unique Skills will *not* contribute to your ultimate meter.
*   **Magic Cost:** Even with a full meter, Ultimates are incredibly "expensive" to cast. Most require between **2 to 4 million Magic**.

If you find that you cannot cast your newly unlocked Ultimate, you likely need to increase your Existence Points (EP). Higher EP directly correlates to a larger Magic pool, allowing you to sustain these high-cost abilities.

## FAQ

**Q: Why didn't my skills upgrade after I evolved my race?**

A: The most common reason is that your skill mastery was not at 100% before you initiated the evolution. The game only upgrades skills that are fully mastered at the time of the racial rank-up.

**Q: How do I get more souls for the Ultimate Skill upgrade?**

A: Souls are primarily obtained by defeating bosses and high-level enemies in dungeons. You will need 10,000 souls for the specific evolution step that unlocks Ultimate Skills.

**Q: What is the fastest way to learn tensura: incremental how to upgrade skills for Ultimates?**

A: Focus on maxing your Unique Skills first through constant combat. Once they are at 100% mastery, save up 10,000 souls and 2-4 million Magic (via EP farming) to ensure you can actually use the skill once it is unlocked through evolution.

**Q: Can I use Racial Skills to charge my Ultimate Meter?**
