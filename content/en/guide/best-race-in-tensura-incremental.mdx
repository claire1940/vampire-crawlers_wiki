## Evolution Requirements and Ultimate Skills

To truly unlock the potential of the **best race in tensura incremental**, you must understand the evolution system. Simply rolling a high-tier race is not enough; you must evolve that race to access Ultimate Skills. These skills are the pinnacle of your character's power and are required for high-level raiding.

### The Path to Evolution
To evolve your race and unlock the second upgrade tier, you must follow a specific progression path:
1. **Master Unique Skills:** All of your unique skills must reach 100% mastery.
2. **Soul Accumulation:** You need to gather **10,000 Souls**.
3. **Magicule Threshold:** Most evolutions require a significant pool of Magicules, often ranging between 2 to 4 million.

Once these conditions are met, the second upgrade tier becomes available. This is where you unlock the "Lord" series of skills, which are currently the strongest abilities in the game.

### Ultimate Skills Breakdown
The race you choose often dictates which Ultimate Skills you can effectively utilize. Because these skills require millions of Magicules, a race like the True Dragon or Demon Peer is essential to provide the necessary energy pool.

## Mastering the Gameplay Loop

Even with the **best race in tensura incremental**, you will struggle if you do not master the basic gameplay loop. Ultimate Skills cannot be spammed; they require you to fill an "Ultimate Meter" by using basic skills. This means your build must include a rotation of low-cost basic skills to keep your meter charging.

> **💡 Tip:** Avoid using racial or spirit skills if your goal is to charge your Ultimate Meter quickly. Only **Basic Skills** contribute to the meter's growth in the current 2026 patch.

To maintain your power, you must also farm Existence Points (EP). EP is the primary metric for your character's strength and directly influences your total Magicule count. The most efficient ways to farm EP in 2026 are:
*   **Raids:** Participate in Hinata or Veldora raids for massive EP drops.
*   **Training Zones:** Use AFK training zones specifically designed for your current evolution tier.
*   **Codes:** Always check for the latest developer codes, as they frequently provide Soul boosts and Magicule multipliers.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best race in tensura incremental for beginners?**

A: While True Dragon is the best overall, beginners should aim for the **Kijin or High Elf**. These races have much higher drop rates (15% and 5% respectively) and provide enough of a stat boost to reach the mid-game where farming for a True Dragon becomes easier.

**Q: How do I unlock Ultimate Skills like Lord of Wrath?**

A: You must first master your Unique Skills to 100%. Then, you must evolve your race using 10,000 Souls. The second evolution upgrade is what grants access to the Ultimate Skills menu, provided you have the required Magicules (2M-4M).

**Q: Is the True Dragon race permanent?**

A: Yes, once you roll a race, it stays with your character until you choose to use a reroll item or prestige and select a new race option. Given its 0.25% rarity, most players never reroll once they land a True Dragon.

**Q: Why is my health lower after the 2026 update?**

A: The 2026 update revamped the health system to make it more balanced. Health is now more expensive to upgrade, and base health stats for most races were adjusted. To compensate, focus on upgrading your existence points (EP) and using defensive Ultimate Skills.
