## How to Unlock and Use Ultimate Skills

The 2026 update has introduced a massive shift in power dynamics through the introduction of Ultimate Skills. These are not handed out freely; they require significant dedication to mastery and resource gathering. To begin your journey toward an Ultimate Skill, you must first reach 100% mastery with your Unique Skills.

Once mastery is achieved, the evolution process begins. You must evolve your race, and the specific "second upgrade"—which currently costs **10,000 Souls**—is the gateway to unlocking your Ultimates.

### Ultimate Skill Mechanics
Unlike basic or racial skills, Ultimates operate on a unique resource system:
1.  **Ultimate Meter:** This must be filled before a skill can be triggered.
2.  **Charging:** The meter only charges when you use **Basic Skills**. Using racial, spirit, or unique skills will not contribute to the meter.
3.  **Magicule Cost:** Activating an Ultimate requires a massive reservoir of Magicules, typically ranging between **2 million and 4 million**.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I enter a tensura: incremental code?**

A: You can enter codes via the in-game menu. Look for the "Codes" or "Twitter" icon (usually located on the side of the screen), type your code into the text box, and press enter to claim your rewards.

**Q: Why isn't my Ultimate Skill meter filling up?**

A: The Ultimate meter only charges when you use **Basic Skills**. If you are using Unique, Racial, or Spirit skills, the meter will remain stagnant. Stick to your primary basic attacks to build energy.

**Q: How do I get more Magicules quickly?**

A: The most effective way to gain Magicules is by increasing your Existence Points (EP) through intensive training sessions and participating in high-level raids. Some codes also provide Magicule boosts.

**Q: What is the best Ultimate Skill for solo players?**

A: **Lord of Chaos** is widely considered the best for solo play due to its balance of high damage, self-buffs, and enemy debuffs. If you find yourself dying too often, **Lord of Purity** is a necessary alternative for its healing capabilities.
