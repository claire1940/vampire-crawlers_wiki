## The Importance of Race Evolution and Skill Mastery

In Tensura Incremental, your race is the foundation of your character's growth. Unlike many other incremental games where races provide static buffs, here, your race is the literal gatekeeper to your skill levels. To become truly powerful, you must understand the relationship between Skill Mastery and Race Evolution.

Before you can upgrade any of your active or passive skills, you must reach 100% Skill Mastery. This is a non-negotiable step in the progression loop. Once you have achieved full mastery, you must then evolve your race to trigger the actual skill upgrade. Without evolving, your skills will remain capped, regardless of how many battles you complete.

| Progression Step | Requirement | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Mastery** | Reach **100% Mastery** via combat | Readies the skill for an upgrade |
| **Race Evolution** | Spend **Existence Points (EP)** or Souls | Upgrades skills and increases base stats |
| **Prestige** | Reach specific **Evolution milestones** | Resets progress for permanent multipliers |

> **💡 Tip:** Never skip the 100% mastery requirement. Evolving your race without having your skills at max mastery can lead to wasted progression opportunities and slower clear times in higher-level dungeons.

## Why Human is One of the Tensura Incremental Best Races

When discussing the **tensura incremental best races** for efficiency, the Human race currently stands at the top of the "Prestige Meta." While other races might offer unique late-game abilities or higher raw stat caps in the deep endgame, the Human race is unparalleled for speedrunning.

The primary advantage of the Human race is the ease of evolution. In the current 2026 update, Humans require fewer resources and less time to move through their evolutionary tiers compared to more exotic races like Slimes or Ogres. This makes them the go-to choice for players who want to farm Prestige points quickly to boost their overall account power.

### The Prestige Speedrun Strategy

To execute a prestige speedrun using the Human race, follow this optimized path:

1.  **Select the Human Race:** Start your run or use a race change to begin as a Human.
2.  **The Falmouth Battle:** Head immediately to the Falmouth raid. This battle is essential because it provides a massive influx of Existence Points (EP) and the 10,000 souls you will eventually need for later stages.
3.  **Rapid Evolution:** Use the EP gained from just one or two Falmouth battles to evolve twice immediately.
4.  **Prestige:** Once you have hit the required evolution tier (which usually takes about 2 minutes of total gameplay), trigger your Prestige.

### Understanding Existence Points (EP)

Existence Points are the "currency" of evolution. Every race requires a different amount of EP to reach its next form. The reason Humans are consistently ranked among the **tensura incremental best races** is that their EP thresholds are significantly lower in the early tiers. This allows a player to "loop" their progress much faster than a player using a high-cost race like a Dragonoid.

> **Warning:** If you are trying to climb the leaderboards, do not get stuck on a "cool" race early on. Focus on the Human race to build your Prestige multipliers first, then switch to a combat-heavy race once your base stats are high enough.

## How to Upgrade Skills Through Evolution

A common point of confusion for new players is why their skills aren't getting stronger despite constant usage. As mentioned, the secret lies in the evolution screen. In [Tensura Incremental on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/14546591040/Tensura-Incremental), your race evolution acts as the "level up" trigger for your entire kit.

### Step-by-Step Skill Upgrade Process

1.  **Monitor Mastery:** Open your skill menu and check the percentage. It must be at **100%**.
2.  **Gather Souls:** Ensure you have the required number of souls (the Falmouth battle is the best source for this).
3.  **Trigger Evolution:** Navigate to the evolution menu. If you meet the EP and Soul requirements, your race will change, and your max-mastery skills will automatically upgrade to their next tier.
4.  **Repeat:** Once evolved, your mastery will reset, allowing you to train the skills again for the next evolution tier.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute best race for new players in Tensura Incremental?**

A: For beginners, the **Human** race is widely considered the best choice. Its low evolution requirements allow you to learn the mechanics of the game and start prestige-looping much faster than other races.

**Q: How do I get more souls for my race evolution?**

A: The most efficient way to farm souls in the current version of the game is the Falmouth battle. One successful run can provide upwards of 10,000 souls, which is enough to cover multiple early-to-mid-game evolutions.

**Q: Can I change my race after I have started?**

A: Yes, you can change your race using race change items or through specific game mechanics. Many players start as a Human to farm Prestige and then switch to a more powerful combat race like a Slime or Demon later on.

**Q: Why are my skills not upgrading after I evolve?**

A: Your skills will only upgrade during evolution if they have reached **100% Mastery** beforehand. If you evolve with only 90% mastery, the skill will remain at its current level, and you will have to wait until your next evolution to try again. This is why tracking your **tensura incremental best races** and their evolution timing is so important.
