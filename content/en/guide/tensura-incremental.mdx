## Mastering Combat: Skills and Magic Sensitivity

Combat in **tensura incremental** is a turn-based strategic affair where your magical reserves and skill selection dictate the outcome. One of the most common mistakes new players make is ignoring their "Magical Regeneration" stat. Without a steady flow of mana, you will find yourself unable to use powerful skills, forced to skip turns while the enemy whittles down your health.

### Essential Skills for 2026

1.  **Steal Strength:** This is a core skill for any melee-focused build. It allows you to weaken the opponent while boosting your own damage output, often leading to one-tap victories against same-grade monsters.
2.  **Black Lightning:** A high-damage magic skill that is effective for clearing Grade A missions quickly.
3.  **Body Armor Magic:** While some players find it niche, it is vital for survival when facing bosses that can one-shot you.
4.  **Dirt Kick:** A useful utility skill for early-game crowd control and turn manipulation.

> **Warning:** If your magical sensitivity is low, high-tier skills like Black Lightning will drain your mana pool instantly. Always balance your "Skill Damage" upgrades with "Magical Regeneration."

## Mission Progression: From Hobgoblins to Flame Giants

Progressing through missions is the only way to increase your Grade and unlock the full potential of your character. Each mission offers a different challenge, and jumping into a Grade A mission too early will result in a quick trip back to the spawn point.

| Mission Grade | Typical Enemy | Recommended Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grade D** | Hobgoblin Soldier | 500+ Health, Basic Melee |
| **Grade A-** | Direwolf Pack | 2000+ Health, Steal Strength |

## Optimizing Your Build: Stats and Regeneration

To dominate the leaderboards in 2026, you must visit the Analyst frequently. The Analyst allows you to spend your earned points on permanent stat increases. While it is tempting to dump everything into Melee Damage, a balanced build is much more effective for long-term progression.

*   **Melee Damage:** Essential for finishing off enemies without consuming mana.
*   **Magic/Skill Damage:** The primary way to deal massive bursts of damage to high-grade bosses.
*   **Health Regen:** Reduces downtime between training sessions and missions.
*   **Magical Regeneration:** The most underrated stat; high regeneration allows you to spam skills every turn.

By focusing on these core areas, you will find that the grind in **tensura incremental** becomes significantly smoother. Keep an eye out for double-bonus events, as these are the best times to push for higher Grades and roll for that elusive True Dragon race.
