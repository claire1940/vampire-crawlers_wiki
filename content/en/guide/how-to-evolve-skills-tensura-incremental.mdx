## Step 1: Achieving 100% Mastery

Before you can even think about **how to evolve skills tensura incremental**, you must demonstrate total proficiency with your current abilities. Every skill in your hotbar, from basic strikes to unique racial traits, has a mastery percentage visible in the skill menu.

*   **How to gain mastery:** You must use the skill repeatedly in active combat. Passive training does not contribute to mastery levels as efficiently as dungeon crawling or boss raiding.
*   **The Threshold:** You cannot trigger an upgrade until the mastery reaches exactly **100%**. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to evolve your race for the purpose of skill upgrades if your current mastery is sitting at 99% or lower. The skill will remain at its current tier, and you will have wasted a significant opportunity for power growth. You will be forced to wait until your next racial evolution to try again.

## Step 2: Racial Evolution as the Catalyst

In Tensura Incremental, your race acts as the "cap" for your skill levels. When you reach the requirements for a racial evolution, the game checks your current skill masteries. If they are maxed out, the evolution process will "awaken" those skills into their next forms. This is the secret to **how to evolve skills tensura incremental** effectively: timing your racial rank-up with your mastery completion.

### How to Use and Charge Ultimate Skills
Ultimate Skills do not operate like your standard attacks. They require a dedicated **Ultimate Meter** to be filled before they can be activated.

1.  **Charging the Meter:** You can only charge your ultimate by using **Basic Skills**. 
2.  **Restrictions:** Using Racial Skills, Spirit Skills, or Unique Skills will *not* contribute to your ultimate meter.
3.  **Magic Cost:** Even with a full meter, Ultimates are incredibly "expensive" to cast. Most require between **2 to 4 million Magic**.

If you find that you cannot cast your newly unlocked Ultimate, you likely need to increase your Existence Points (EP). Higher EP directly correlates to a larger Magic pool, allowing you to sustain these high-cost abilities. You can track your progress and find community help on the [official Roblox Tensura Incremental page](https://www.roblox.com/games/tensura-incremental).

## FAQ

**Q: Why didn't my skills upgrade after I evolved my race?**

A: The most common reason is that your skill mastery was not at 100% before you initiated the racial evolution. The game only "awakens" skills that are fully mastered at the exact moment of the racial rank-up. If you were at 99%, the skill remains at its previous tier.

**Q: How do I get more souls for the Ultimate Skill upgrade?**

A: Souls are primarily obtained by defeating bosses and high-level enemies in dungeons. For the 2026 update, farming the mid-tier dungeon floors is the most efficient way to reach the 10,000 souls required for Ultimate Skill unlocking.

**Q: What is the fastest way to learn how to evolve skills tensura incremental for Ultimates?**

A: Focus on maxing your Unique Skills first through constant combat. Once they are at 100% mastery, save up 10,000 souls and 2-4 million Magic (via EP farming) to ensure you can actually use the skill once it is unlocked through the racial evolution tree.

**Q: Can I use Racial Skills to charge my Ultimate Meter?**

A: No. The Ultimate Meter only charges when you use Basic Skills. Racial, Spirit, and Unique skills do not contribute to the charge, though they are still vital for dealing damage during the cooldown periods of your basic attacks.
