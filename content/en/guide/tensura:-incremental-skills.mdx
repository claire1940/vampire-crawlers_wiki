## Understanding the Skill Mastery System

Before you can think about devastating your enemies with high-tier magic, you must first master the basics. In *Tensura: Incremental*, every skill has a mastery percentage that tracks your proficiency. You cannot simply bypass this grind if you want to evolve.

To upgrade any skill, your current mastery must be at exactly **100%**. Skipping this step or attempting to evolve without full mastery will result in a stagnant build. Mastery is generally gained by consistent use of the skill in combat or during training sessions. Once you hit that 100% threshold, the skill is "primed" for its next form.

| Skill Type | Mastery Requirement | Primary Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Skills** | 100% | Charging the Ultimate Meter and early-game DPS |
| **Unique Skills** | 100% | Providing character-specific utility and passives |
| **Racial Skills** | 100% | Stat buffs and abilities tied to your current evolution |
| **Ultimate Skills** | 100% | High-cost, high-damage abilities for endgame content |

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to spend resources on evolution until you have verified that your primary unique skills have reached 100% mastery. Failing to do so can delay your transition to Ultimate-tier gameplay.

## How to Upgrade Tensura: Incremental Skills

Upgrading your abilities is intrinsically tied to your race. Unlike other RPGs where you might simply click an "upgrade" button in a menu, here, your physical evolution is what triggers the enhancement of your kit.

1.  **Reach 100% Mastery:** As mentioned, this is the non-negotiable first step for all **tensura: incremental skills**.
2.  **Gather Evolution Materials:** Depending on your current tier, you may need specific items from dungeons or a set amount of Existence Points (EP).
3.  **Evolve Your Race:** When you evolve to a higher racial tier (e.g., from Ogre to Kijin), your mastered skills will automatically transition into their upgraded versions.

This system ensures that your power level remains consistent with your character's lore-friendly progression. It is a simple mechanic, but many players miss it by focusing solely on combat and forgetting to check their evolution requirements.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills

The 2026 update cycle introduced a revamped Ultimate Skill system, which represents the pinnacle of power in the game. These skills are not easily obtained and require a significant investment of time and resources. 

To unlock an Ultimate Skill, you must first max out your **Unique Skills** to 100% mastery. Following this, you must proceed with a specific racial evolution that includes a secondary upgrade path. This specific upgrade carries a heavy price tag: **10,000 Souls**. Souls are a premium currency earned through high-level play, making the decision of which Ultimate to unlock a critical one.

### The Ultimate Meter Mechanics

Using an Ultimate Skill isn't as simple as waiting for a cooldown. You must actively charge your **Ultimate Meter** during battle. Interestingly, the game restricts how you can build this charge:
*   **Basic Skills Only:** Only your standard, basic skills contribute to the meter.
*   **Excluded Skills:** Using Racial skills, Spirit skills, or Unique skills will **not** charge the meter.

This creates a tactical loop where players must balance using their weaker basic attacks to build up for a massive Ultimate strike. Furthermore, these skills consume a massive amount of Magicules—typically between **2 to 4 million** per cast.

## Ultimate Skills Comparison Table

Choosing which Ultimate to prioritize depends on your role in a party. Some skills focus on raw damage, while others provide essential utility for raids.

| Ultimate Skill | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Wrath** | Extreme Burst Damage | Bossing and single-target elimination |

## Farming Existence Points and Magicules

To support your **tensura: incremental skills**, you need a massive pool of Magicules. Your Magicule capacity is determined by your **Existence Points (EP)**. If your EP is too low, you won't even be able to cast your Ultimate Skills, regardless of whether you have unlocked them.

### Best Farming Methods:
1.  **Training:** The most consistent way to gain EP for solo players. Use the dedicated training zones to slowly build your base stats.
2.  **Raids:** High-risk, high-reward. Raids provide the fastest EP gains and are essential for reaching the 4 million Magicule threshold required for top-tier Ultimates.
3.  **Dungeons:** Good for secondary materials and souls, though slightly less efficient for pure EP than dedicated raids.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I unlock my Ultimate Skill even though I have 10,000 souls?**

A: You must ensure two things: first, that your Unique Skills have reached 100% mastery, and second, that you have evolved your race to the specific tier that allows for the Ultimate upgrade. Check your evolution tree to see if you have met the prerequisite racial form.

**Q: What is the fastest way to charge the Ultimate Meter in Tensura: Incremental?**

A: The only way to charge the meter is by using basic skills. To charge it quickly, focus on high-frequency basic attacks and avoid using your Racial or Spirit skills until the meter is full, as those do not contribute to the charge.

**Q: How do I increase my maximum Magicules?**

A: Your Magicule limit is tied to your Existence Points (EP). To increase your EP, you should participate in raids or spend time in the training grounds. Higher EP allows you to hold the 2 to 4 million Magicules required for Ultimate abilities.

**Q: Is Disintegration better than Lord of Wrath?**

A: In the current 2026 meta, Lord of Wrath is superior for raw damage. While Disintegration deals decent damage, it does not scale as well as Lord of Wrath, which can currently take out nearly half of a high-level boss's HP in one hit.
