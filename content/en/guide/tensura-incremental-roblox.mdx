## Getting Started: The Path from Grade F to A

Every journey in Tensura Incremental begins with the basics. When you first spawn, your character will be at Grade F or E, possessing very little health and minimal magicules. Your primary goal is to increase your Grade by accumulating Experience (XP) and Existence Points (EP). This is primarily achieved through the training menu, which offers different regimens based on your current stats.

To progress efficiently, you must balance your training types. Focusing solely on one stat will leave you vulnerable in missions. For instance, high melee damage is useless if your health regeneration is too low to survive a single turn against a Hobgoblin Soldier.

| Training Type | Primary Reward | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Conditioning** | XP and Melee Stats | Early game leveling and physical builds |
| **Endurance Training** | Health and Defense | Increasing survivability against bosses |
| **Combat Drills** | 250 EP / 100 XP | Balanced progression and EP farming |
| **Spiritual Meditation** | 500 EP / 225 XP | Magic damage and magicule generation |

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize upgrading your "Magicule Generation" and "Health Regen" in the Analyst menu early on. This reduces downtime between training sessions and missions.

## Mastering the Combat and Mission System

Combat in Tensura Incremental is a turn-based affair that relies heavily on your magicule pool. If you run out of magicules, you will be unable to use skills, often forcing you to skip turns or flee. Most beginners make the mistake of jumping into Grade D missions too early. If you find yourself losing, return to training until you can comfortably "one-tap" enemies in your current grade.

### Key Combat Skills for Beginners
1.  **Steal Strength:** A vital early-game skill that can deal significant damage while potentially weakening the opponent.
2.  **Body Armor Magic:** Useful for blocking incoming damage, though it should be replaced once your health pool is large enough to tank hits.
3.  **Black Lightning/Flame:** These are excellent mid-tier skills for clearing Grade A missions like the Flame Giant.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills

The 2026 updates have introduced a revamped UI and the highly anticipated Ultimate Skills. These are the pinnacle of power in Tensura Incremental, but they are not easily obtained. To even begin the process, you must have 100% mastery on all of your Unique Skills.

Once mastery is achieved, the path to Ultimates involves race evolution. You will need to evolve your race and then purchase the second upgrade tier, which typically costs **10,000 Souls**. Souls are a rare currency earned through high-level progression and specific raids.

### How Ultimate Skills Work
Unlike basic skills, Ultimate Skills require a charged "Ultimate Meter." This meter can **only** be charged by using Basic Skills in combat. Using racial skills, spirit skills, or unique skills will not contribute to the meter. Furthermore, activating an ultimate requires a massive amount of magicules—usually between 2 million and 4 million.

| Ultimate Skill | Effect Type | Performance Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Wrath** | DPS | Currently the highest damage skill; can half-HP bosses. |
| **Lord of Purity** | Healing | The premier support skill for long-duration raids. |
| **Lord of Void** | DPS / Debuff | Deals heavy damage while weakening enemy defenses. |
| **Lord of Chaos** | Balanced | Provides self-buffs while damaging and debuffing enemies. |
| **Lord of Powers** | Support | Grants massive buffs to all stats; ideal for team play. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The 2026 health system overhaul has made HP more expensive to upgrade. You may notice your total health is lower than in previous versions; prioritize "Existence Points" to compensate for this change.

## Existence Points (EP) and Magicule Management

Existence Points serve as the primary indicator of your overall power level. To increase your maximum magicules—which is necessary to cast the Ultimate Skills mentioned above—you must farm EP relentlessly. The most effective way to do this in 2026 is through a combination of **Spiritual Meditation** and participating in **Raids**.

Raids provide a significantly higher EP yield than solo training but require a competent build. Before joining a raid, ensure your "Magical Sensitivity" and "Aura Stabilization" are fully upgraded in the Analyst tab. This ensures you aren't wasting the magicules you generate during the heat of battle.

## Advanced Progression Strategy

As you reach the late game, your focus should shift from XP to Soul farming and Mastery.
1.  **Maxing Mastery:** Use your unique skills repeatedly in lower-level missions to hit 100% mastery quickly without the risk of dying.
2.  **Soul Accumulation:** Focus on Grade A+ missions and world bosses. You need 10,000 souls for the ultimate unlock, so do not spend them on minor stat boosts early on.
3.  **UI Utilization:** Use the new 2026 UI icons to track your skill cooldowns more effectively. The cleaner interface allows you to see your Magicule regeneration rate in real-time, which is crucial for timing your Ultimates.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I charge my Ultimate Meter in Tensura Incremental Roblox?**

A: You can only charge the Ultimate Meter by using Basic Skills during combat. Using Unique, Racial, or Spirit skills will not increase the meter. Once the meter is full and you have the required 2-4 million magicules, you can trigger your Ultimate.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Existence Points (EP)?**

A: For solo players, Spiritual Meditation is the most efficient training method for EP. However, participating in Raids is the fastest overall method, provided you have the stats to survive the encounter.

**Q: Why is my health lower after the latest update?**

A: The 2026 update revamped the health system to make it more balanced. Health is now more expensive to upgrade, and base values were adjusted. To regain your previous tankiness, you must invest more heavily into the health stat via the Analyst.

**Q: How do I evolve my race?**

A: Race evolution becomes available once you hit specific Grade milestones and accumulate enough XP. You will need to access the evolution menu and spend the required resources (usually Souls or high amounts of EP) to move to the next racial tier.
