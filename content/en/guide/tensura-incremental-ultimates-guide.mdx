## The Prerequisites: Preparing for Ultimate Power

Before you can even think about wielding the Lord of Wrath or the Lord of Void, you must satisfy several strict requirements. The progression system in Tensura Incremental is strictly linear, meaning you cannot skip steps or "luck" your way into an ultimate skill. 

The first and most vital step is **Skill Mastery**. Every unique skill in your current arsenal must reach 100% mastery. This is achieved through consistent use in combat and training. If even one unique skill is sitting at 99%, the option to evolve will remain locked. Once your mastery is capped, you must focus on your racial evolution.

| Requirement | Threshold | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unique Skill Mastery** | **100%** | Prerequisite for racial evolution and skill transformation. |
| **Soul Count** | **10,000 Souls** | Required for the second stage of racial evolution to unlock Ultimates. |
| **Existence Points (EP)** | **Variable** | Increases your Magicule capacity to handle Ultimate costs. |
| **Race Level** | **Max Evolved** | Necessary to access the Ultimate Skill menu. |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not ignore your basic skills during the mastery grind. While unique skills are more powerful, your ultimate skills will eventually rely on your basic skill usage to charge their meters.

## How to Unlock Ultimate Skills

Unlocking your first ultimate is a two-step process involving racial evolution. After you have maxed out your unique skill mastery, you must navigate to the evolution menu. The initial evolution changes your base stats and appearance, but it is the **second upgrade** within the evolution tree that is the "game-changer."

This second upgrade carries a heavy price tag of **10,000 souls**. Souls are primarily gathered by defeating high-level mobs and participating in boss raids. Once you pay this toll, the "Ultimate" tab in your skill menu will become accessible.

### The Magicule Barrier
Even after unlocking the skills, you may find yourself unable to cast them. Ultimate skills in 2026 have massive Magicule requirements, often ranging between **2 million and 4 million Magicules** per cast. To reach these numbers, you must farm **Existence Points (EP)**. 

EP acts as a multiplier for your internal energy reserves. You can farm EP through:
1.  **AFK Training:** Slow but steady growth for casual players.
2.  **Raids:** High-risk, high-reward encounters that provide massive EP chunks.
3.  **Dungeon Clears:** Essential for obtaining items that boost EP gain efficiency.

## Ultimate Skill Mechanics: Charging the Meter

One of the most common points of confusion for players is the Ultimate Meter. Unlike basic or unique skills that operate on a standard cooldown, Ultimate skills require you to build up energy during combat.

The 2026 update narrowed the charging mechanics significantly. To fill your ultimate meter, you **must use basic skills**. 
*   **Basic Skills:** Charge the meter.
*   **Racial Skills:** Do NOT charge the meter.
*   **Spirit Skills:** Do NOT charge the meter.
*   **Unique Skills:** Do NOT charge the meter.

This creates a specific combat rhythm where you must weave basic attacks between your heavy hitters to ensure your Ultimate is ready for the final phase of a boss fight.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Because your health is lower in the current version, the "glass cannon" build (focusing entirely on damage) is riskier than ever. Balancing your EP between Magicules and defensive stats is crucial for surviving ultimate-level encounters.

To compensate for lower health, you should look for the [official Tensura Incremental community](https://www.roblox.com/games/14532414175/Tensura-Incremental) to stay updated on the latest codes, which often provide health boosters or soul multipliers to help bridge the gap.

## FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my ultimate meter increasing during combat?**

A: You are likely using unique, racial, or spirit skills. In the current version of the game, only **basic skills** contribute to the ultimate meter charge. Switch your rotation to include more basic attacks to fix this.

**Q: Is 10,000 souls the final cost for all ultimates?**

A: The 10,000 souls are required for the racial evolution that unlocks the *ability* to get ultimates. Individual skills may have additional requirements or mastery checks, but the soul cost is the primary barrier for the **tensura incremental ultimates guide** progression.

**Q: Which ultimate should I unlock first for solo play?**

A: **Lord of Chaos** or **Lord of Void** are generally recommended for solo players. They provide a balance of damage and debuffs that allow you to survive encounters while still dealing enough damage to clear content.

**Q: How do I get more Magicules quickly?**

A: Magicule capacity is tied to your Existence Points (EP). The fastest way to gain EP is through high-level raids. If you are not strong enough for raids yet, focus on AFK training with EP-boosting items equipped.

**Q: Did they nerf the health system in the latest update?**

A: Yes, the health system was revamped to be more expensive, resulting in lower total HP for most players. This makes healing ultimates like Lord of Purity and defensive buffs much more important in the 2026 meta.
