## Getting Started: Early Game Progression

When you first spawn into the world, your primary goal is to increase your Grade. You begin at the bottom (Grade F/E) and must use the "Training" tab to build your foundation. Early on, focus on **Basic Conditioning** to build your initial XP and EP (Existence Points).

As your Grade improves, you unlock more efficient training methods. Transitioning from basic drills to spiritual meditation is the fastest way to gain the resources needed for your first race evolution.

| Training Method | XP Gain | EP Gain | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Conditioning** | Low | Low | Grades F to E; early stat padding |
| **Combat Drills** | Medium | Medium | Unlocking early melee and magic skills |
| **Endurance Training** | Medium | High | Boosting Health and Defense stats |
| **Spiritual Meditation** | **High** | **Very High** | Farming Magicules and EP for late-game |

> **Warning:** Do not attempt Grade A missions until you have invested significantly into Health Regen and Magic Damage. Many players find themselves "one-tapped" by bosses if they ignore defensive stats in the early game.

## Understanding Stats and Upgrades

The "Analyst" tab is where you customize your build. While it is tempting to dump all points into Melee Damage, a balanced approach is required to handle the diverse enemy types found in missions.

*   **Melee Damage:** Essential for finishing off enemies when your magicules run dry.
*   **Magic/Skill Damage:** The primary source of DPS for most builds, especially those utilizing Black Flame or Black Lightning.
*   **Magical Generation:** Perhaps the most underrated stat; it dictates how quickly you can use skills consecutively.
*   **Health & Regen:** Vital for surviving the revamped 2026 health system, which is more punishing than previous versions.

## How to Unlock Ultimate Skills

Ultimate skills represent the pinnacle of power in Tensura Incremental. They are not simply "bought" but earned through a specific progression path. To begin this journey, you must first reach 100% mastery on your **Unique Skills**.

1.  **Max Mastery:** Ensure your unique skills are fully leveled through repeated use in combat.
2.  **Race Evolution:** Evolve your character's race to the required tier.
3.  **The Soul Cost:** Purchase the second upgrade in the evolution tree, which typically costs **10,000 Souls**.
4.  **Magicule Threshold:** Be prepared to farm between 2 to 4 million Magicules to actually cast these abilities in battle.

### Ultimate Skills Tier List

| Skill Name | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Wrath** | **DPS** | Highest raw damage in the game; massive HP shredder. |
| **Lord of Purity** | **Heal** | The premier healing skill for soloing high-level raids. |
| **Lord of Chaos** | **Hybrid** | Deals significant damage while applying debuffs to enemies. |
| **Lord of Void** | **Debuff** | Strong damage coupled with powerful crowd control effects. |
| **Lord of Powers** | **Support** | Provides massive stat buffs to the user and allies. |

> **Tip:** To charge your Ultimate Meter, you must use **Basic Skills**. Unique, Racial, and Spirit skills do not contribute to the ultimate charge, so keep your basic rotations active.

## Advanced Combat Strategies

Once you reach Grade A and beyond, combat becomes a tactical turn-based affair. Using skills like **Body Armor Magic** can mitigate incoming damage, while **Steal Strength** is excellent for weakening bosses like the Hobgoblin Soldier or Flame Giant.

If you find yourself struggling with magicule costs, prioritize upgrading your **Magical Sensitivity** and **Aura Stabilization**. These allow you to stay in the fight longer without having to skip turns to recharge. ## Mission and Farming Tiers

Farming missions is the most reliable way to gain XP outside of training. Always aim for missions that match your current Grade for optimal rewards.

*   **Grade D - C:** Focus on Hobgoblin Soldiers. They are predictable and easy to "one-tap" with basic strength-stealing builds.
*   **Grade B - A:** Flame Giants and high-tier monsters provide the Souls necessary for the 10,000 Soul ultimate requirement.
*   **S Rank & Raids:** These require Ultimate Skills and high EP. Do not attempt these solo without **Lord of Purity** or **Lord of Wrath**.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I charge my ultimate meter in Tensura Incremental?**

A: You can only charge the meter by using Basic Skills. Racial, Spirit, and Unique skills will not contribute to the meter, so ensure your rotation includes basic attacks.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get EP (Existence Points)?**

A: Spiritual Meditation is the most effective training method for EP. Later in the game, participating in Raids becomes the primary source of high-volume EP.

**Q: Why is my health lower than it used to be?**

A: The 2026 UI and balance update revamped the health system. Health is now more expensive to upgrade, and base values have been adjusted, making defensive stats and health regeneration more critical than in previous versions.

**Q: Which ultimate skill should I unlock first?**

A: According to this **tensura incremental guide**, Lord of Wrath is recommended for players who want to clear content quickly due to its high damage. However, if you prefer survivability, Lord of Purity is the best choice for its unparalleled healing capabilities.
