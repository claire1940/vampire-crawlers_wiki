## Understanding Skill Mastery and Evolution

The foundation of any powerful build in Tensura Incremental begins with mastery. Unlike other RPGs where skills might level up automatically, this game requires a dedicated focus on "Mastery Percentage." You cannot progress to the higher tiers of power without first perfecting your current kit.

### The 100% Mastery Rule

Before you can even think about upgrading or evolving your skills, you must reach 100% mastery with your current unique skills. This is a non-negotiable requirement. Skipping this step or attempting to evolve early will often result in a "stuck" progression loop where your character lacks the necessary power to farm the resources required for the next tier.

| Requirement | Value | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unique Skill Mastery** | 100% | Unlocks the ability to evolve race and skills |
| **Soul Cost (Tier 2)** | 10,000 | Required for the final Ultimate unlock evolution |
| **Magicule Threshold** | 2M - 4M | Necessary to cast Ultimate abilities |
| **Existence Points (EP)** | Variable | Increases total Magicule capacity |

> **💡 Tip:** To grind mastery quickly, focus on consistent training sessions rather than high-difficulty raids. Consistency is more rewarded than burst damage when it comes to mastery gains.

## How to Unlock Ultimate Skills

The pinnacle of the **tensura incremental skills guide** is the acquisition of Ultimate Skills. These are not simply "stronger" versions of your current moves; they are game-changing abilities that can turn the tide of a boss battle in seconds. However, the path to unlocking them is rigorous.

1.  **Max Out Unique Skills:** Ensure every unique skill in your current inventory has reached 100% mastery.
2.  **Racial Evolution:** You must evolve your race through the character menu. This usually requires specific items or level milestones.
3.  **The Second Upgrade:** Once evolved, you need to purchase the second upgrade in the evolution tree. This specific upgrade costs **10,000 Souls** and is the literal "key" that transforms your unique skills into Ultimate skills.

### Managing the Ultimate Meter

Using these skills isn't as simple as waiting for a cooldown. You must actively charge your Ultimate Meter. A common mistake players make is trying to use Racial or Spirit skills to charge this meter. **Only Basic Skills charge the Ultimate Meter.** 

This creates a specific combat rhythm: you must cycle through your basic attacks and low-level skills to build energy, then unleash your Ultimate when the Magicule count is sufficient. Since Ultimates cost between 2 million and 4 million Magicules, you cannot spam them.

## Resource Farming: Magicules and Existence Points

To sustain the high cost of Ultimate skills, you must focus on your Existence Points (EP). In Tensura Incremental, your EP directly correlates to your Magicule capacity and regeneration rate.

*   **Training:** The primary way to slowly but surely increase your EP. 
*   **Raids:** The high-risk, high-reward method. Participating in successful raids provides massive EP boosts and the Souls necessary for evolution.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The 2026 health system update has made HP much more expensive to maintain. Do not neglect your defensive stats while chasing high Magicule counts, or you will find yourself "glass-cannoning" through content you can no longer survive.

## Summary of Skill Progression

To wrap up this **tensura incremental skills guide**, let's look at the ideal progression path for a player entering the mid-to-late game:

1.  **Level Basics:** Use basic skills to farm low-level mobs and build initial mastery.
2.  **Unique Phase:** Unlock your Unique skills and grind them to 100% mastery.
3.  **Soul Farming:** Participate in raids until you have 10,000 Souls saved up.
4.  **Evolution:** Evolve your race and purchase the Ultimate Skill unlock upgrade.
5.  **EP Grinding:** Focus on training to increase your Magicule pool to at least 4 million.
6.  **Master the Cycle:** Practice the "Basic Skill -> Charge Meter -> Ultimate Burst" combat loop.

By following these steps, you will transform from a lowly slime or goblin into a true Demon Lord capable of challenging the toughest content the game has to offer.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I unlock Ultimate skills even though I have enough Souls?**

A: You must ensure that your Unique skills are at 100% mastery first. Additionally, the Ultimate unlock is specifically the *second* upgrade in the racial evolution tree, not the first.

**Q: What is the fastest way to charge the Ultimate Meter?**

A: As mentioned in this **tensura incremental skills guide**, only basic skills charge the meter. Use your fastest-recovering basic skills in a constant rotation to fill the bar as quickly as possible. Racial and Spirit skills will not contribute to the charge.

**Q: Is Lord of Wrath better than Lord of Chaos?**

A: It depends on your needs. Lord of Wrath deals significantly more raw damage, making it better for speed-running bosses. However, Lord of Chaos provides utility and debuffs that are often more valuable in team-based raids where survival is more difficult.

**Q: How do I get more Magicules for my skills?**

A: You need to increase your Existence Points (EP). This is done through consistent training or by participating in raids. Higher EP increases your maximum Magicule limit, allowing you to cast the more expensive Ultimate abilities that cost up to 4 million Magicules.
