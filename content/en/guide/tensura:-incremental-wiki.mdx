## Resource Management and Optimization

In any incremental game, the goal is to reach a state of "infinite" growth. In Tensura: Incremental, this is achieved through the synergy of Magicules, Essence, and Souls.

1.  **Magicules:** The primary currency. Used for basic upgrades and purchasing new skills.
2.  **Essence:** A secondary currency earned through "Prestige" (resetting your Magicules). Used for permanent talent tree upgrades.
3.  **Souls:** The rarest resource, obtained through boss drops or high-level resets. Necessary for the final evolution stages like True Dragon.

The most effective strategy for 2026 involves a "Hybrid AFK" approach. During active play, focus on completing quests and using the Beelzebuth ultimate. Before going AFK, switch your Ultimates to Raphael to ensure your skills continue to fire and generate resources while you are away. ## Advanced Strategies for the True Dragon Stage

Reaching the True Dragon stage is not the end of the game; it is effectively the beginning of the "Infinite Tier." At this point, your focus shifts from simple evolution to "Aura Refinement" and "Skill Awakening." 

Aura Refinement allows you to sink trillions of Magicules into a single stat, providing diminishing returns that eventually break through the game's hardest content. Skill Awakening, on the other hand, requires specific "Awakening Shards" found in the late-game dungeons. This process turns standard skills into their "God-tier" counterparts, often adding screen-clearing effects and massive multipliers.

| Upgrade Type | Cost Scale | Impact |

| **Skill Awakening** | Fixed (Shards) | Massive power spike for specific abilities. |
