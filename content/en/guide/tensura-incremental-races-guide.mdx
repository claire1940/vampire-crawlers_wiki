## The Foundation of Race Evolution

In Tensura Incremental, progression is tied directly to your ability to evolve. Unlike traditional RPGs where you simply level up, this game requires you to meet specific mastery thresholds before you can transcend your current form. The evolution process typically involves two major components: Skill Mastery and Resource Accumulation.

To begin any evolution, you must ensure that your current skills are fully mastered. This means reaching 100% mastery on your unique skills before the game will even allow you to look at the next racial tier. Once mastery is achieved, you will need to spend Souls—a currency earned through combat and specific quests—to trigger the transformation.

| Evolution Stage | Mastery Required | Soul Cost (Approx) | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Form** | 0% | 0 | Starting stats and basic skills |
| **First Evolution** | 100% Basic Mastery | 1,000 - 5,000 | Enhanced racial traits |
| **Second Evolution** | 100% Unique Mastery | 10,000 | Access to Ultimate Skill slots |
| **Transcendent** | 100% Ultimate Mastery | 50,000+ | Massive Magicule capacity increase |

> **💡 Tip:** Never attempt to evolve before hitting 100% mastery. While the UI might occasionally let you click the button, skipping mastery often results in "bricked" builds where your skills don't scale properly in the next tier.

Once purchased, you gain the ability to equip and use "Lord" tier skills. However, simply having the skill isn't enough; you must also manage your Magicules, which serve as the mana pool for these devastating abilities.

## Resource Management: Magicules and Existence Points

As you progress through the **tensura incremental races guide**, you will notice that your power is strictly limited by your Magicule count. In the 2026 meta, Ultimate Skills require anywhere from 2 to 4 million Magicules per activation.

To increase your maximum Magicule capacity, you must farm Existence Points (EP). EP represents your overall "threat level" in the world of Tensura and can be acquired through two primary methods:
1.  **Training:** Passive or active clicking in training zones.
2.  **Raids:** Participating in group boss battles which provide a significant EP multiplier.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The recent UI update changed the health system. HP is now significantly more expensive to upgrade, and players may find themselves with roughly half the health they had in previous versions. Prioritize defensive racial traits if you plan on soloing raids.

## How to Charge the Ultimate Meter

Evolving your race gives you the *capacity* for power, but combat effectiveness depends on how you use your meter. A common mistake players make after reading a **tensura incremental races guide** is trying to use racial or spirit skills to charge their Ultimates.

In the current version of Tensura Incremental, **only basic skills** charge the ultimate meter.
*   **Basic Skills:** Fast charging, low damage.
*   **Racial Skills:** No charge, medium damage.
*   **Spirit Skills:** No charge, utility focus.
*   **Unique Skills:** No charge, high damage.

This means that even as a high-tier evolved race, you must maintain a rotation of basic attacks to ensure your Ultimate Skill is ready for boss encounters.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I evolve my race even though I have enough souls?**

A: The most common reason is skill mastery. You must ensure all current skills (Basic or Unique, depending on your tier) are at 100% mastery. Check your skill menu and look for any skills sitting at 99% or lower.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Magicules in Tensura Incremental?**

A: Magicules are tied to your Existence Points (EP). The fastest way to increase your cap is by participating in Raids. Following a **tensura incremental races guide** will show you that training is good for AFK sessions, but active Raiding provides the highest EP growth per hour.

**Q: Which Ultimate Skill should I choose first?**

A: If you are playing solo, **Lord of Wrath** is highly recommended due to its massive burst damage, which helps in clearing bosses like Hinata. If you struggle with survivability, **Lord of Purity** is the best healing option available in the 2026 update.

**Q: Does my race affect my health pool?**

A: Yes. Each evolution tier increases your base HP scaling. However, be aware that the 2026 update made HP upgrades more expensive, so you will need to farm significantly more than in previous versions to reach the same tankiness.
