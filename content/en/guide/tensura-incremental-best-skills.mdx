## How to Upgrade and Master Your Skills

Many players make the mistake of thinking skills level up just by using them. While usage increases mastery, the actual "tier" or power level of the skill is often gated behind your character's evolution. To maximize your **tensura incremental best skills**, you must follow a specific progression path.

1.  **Achieve 100% Skill Mastery:** You must use the skill repeatedly until the mastery bar is completely filled.
2.  **Focus on Race Evolution:** Once mastery is maxed, you must evolve your race (e.g., from Goblin to Hobgoblin). This evolution acts as the catalyst that "awakens" your skills to their next level.
3.  **Monitor Prestige Requirements:** Unlike early versions of the game, you do not need to max every single skill to prestige. Focus on your primary race upgrade to progress faster.

## Advanced Progression: The Path to Prestige

As you reach the mid-to-late game in 2026, you will encounter the Prestige system. This resets some of your progress in exchange for permanent multipliers. A common misconception is that you need to max out every skill before hitting the prestige button. 

Current expert strategies suggest that you should focus on **Race Upgrades** above all else. Once your race is fully evolved for your current tier, the benefits of prestiging far outweigh the time spent grinding 100% mastery on every minor Common skill. By focusing on the **tensura incremental best skills** like **Haki** or **Black Flame**, you can maintain high clear speeds even after a reset.

## Intrinsic Skills You Shouldn't Ignore

While Unique skills get all the attention, Intrinsic skills are passive powers that are always active. These are tied to your race but can be upgraded through evolution. For more information on the official lore behind these abilities, you can visit the [Tensura Wiki](https://tensura.fandom.com/wiki/That_Time_I_Got_Reincarnated_as_a_Slime_Wiki) to see how they compare to the source material.

*   **Infinite Regeneration (S-Tier):** Found primarily in the Slime and True Demon Lord paths. It allows you to recover health almost instantly.
*   **Haki (S-Tier):** A powerful aura that debuffs nearby enemies and increases your intimidation factor, often preventing weaker mobs from attacking.
*   **Dragon Scales (S-Tier):** Provides a flat damage reduction percentage that is vital for late-game boss encounters.
*   **Aura Blade (A-Tier):** Infuses your physical attacks with magical energy, allowing you to hit incorporeal enemies.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the absolute tensura incremental best skills for beginners?**

A: For beginners, the best skills are **Meditate** (Common) for mana regeneration and **Self-Regeneration** (Extra) for survivability. If you are lucky enough to roll **Great Sage** as a Unique skill, keep it at all costs as it automates many of the game's complex systems.

**Q: Do I need to max out all skills before I evolve my race?**

A: No, you do not need to max every skill. However, you should ensure that the skills you use most frequently are at 100% mastery before you evolve. The evolution process is what actually upgrades the tier of your mastered skills.

**Q: How do I get S-tier races like Vampire or Daemon?**

A: Races are obtained through rolling at the start of the game or using reroll items (often purchased with in-game currency or gems). S-tier races have a much lower drop rate, so it may take several attempts to land a top-tier species.

**Q: Is "Gluttony" better than "Great Sage" in Tensura Incremental?**

A: In the current 2026 meta, **Great Sage** is generally considered superior because it provides long-term progression benefits and information. **Gluttony** is excellent for raw stat growth, but it requires more active play to be effective compared to the passive power of Great Sage.
