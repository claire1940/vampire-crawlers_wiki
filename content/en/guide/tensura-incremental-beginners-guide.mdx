## The Very Beginning: Choosing Your Path

When you first spawn into the world of Tensura Incremental, you are greeted with a race selection menu. Your choice here significantly impacts the difficulty of your early-game progression. Most players start as a Slime or Goblin, but regardless of your choice, you will be granted a random **Unique Skill**. These skills are the backbone of your build and define your playstyle—whether you become a high-DPS warrior or a supportive tactician.

### Early Game Priorities
Your immediate goal is to increase your **Existence Points (EP)** and **Max Magicules (MP)**. You achieve this by hunting mobs in the starting zones. By default, you gain approximately 3% of a mob's EP upon defeat. 

| Stat Type | Purpose | Progression Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Existence Points (EP)** | Determines your overall power level and rank. | Killing mobs, completing raids, and training. |
| **Magicules (MP)** | The resource consumed to use powerful skills. | Gained alongside EP; higher for Majin races. |
| **Ability Points (AP)** | Used for physical-based combat scaling. | Gained alongside EP; higher for Non-Majin races. |

## The Core Mechanics of Skill Progression

One of the most frequent points of confusion for new players is how to actually upgrade their abilities. In Tensura Incremental, skills do not level up automatically. They are tied directly to your biological "rank" or racial evolution.

### Step 1: Achieving 100% Mastery
Before a skill can evolve, you must demonstrate total proficiency. Every skill in your hotbar has a mastery percentage that increases as you use the skill in active combat. You **must** reach exactly 100% mastery for a skill to be eligible for an upgrade during your next evolution.

> **Warning:** Never initiate a racial evolution if your primary skills are at 99% mastery or lower. If you evolve before hitting 100%, those skills will remain at their current tier, effectively wasting a massive power spike.

### Step 2: Racial Evolution
Your race acts as a "cap" for your skill levels. Once you meet the EP and Soul requirements for an evolution, the game checks your current masteries. If they are maxed, the evolution "awakens" those skills into their next, more powerful forms.

## Unlocking Ultimate Skills in 2026

Ultimate Skills represent the pinnacle of power in the 2026 update. Unlocking them is a multi-step process that requires significant resource investment. To begin, ensure all your **Unique Skills** have reached 100% mastery. 

To trigger an Ultimate Skill unlock, you typically need to purchase the **second upgrade** within your evolution tree. This specific step currently costs approximately **10,000 Souls**. Souls are primarily obtained by defeating dungeon bosses and high-level elites.

### Charging and Casting Ultimates
Ultimate Skills do not function like standard attacks. They require a dedicated **Ultimate Meter** to be filled before activation.

*   **Charging:** The meter only charges when you use **Basic Skills**. Racial, Spirit, and Unique skills do not contribute to the charge.
*   **Magic Cost:** Casting an Ultimate is incredibly expensive, often requiring between **2 to 4 million Magic**. 
*   **EP Requirement:** If you cannot cast your Ultimate despite having a full meter, you likely need to farm more EP to increase your total Magic capacity.

| Ultimate Skill | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lord of Wrath** | Massive Single-Target Burst | Boss shredding and Raid DPS. |
| **Lord of Chaos** | Damage + Buffs + Debuffs | General farming and solo progression. |
| **Lord of Void** | High Damage + Crowd Control | Clearing elite mobs in Dungeons. |
| **Disintegration** | Moderate AoE Damage | Fast wave clearing for low-level mobs. |

## Equipment and Crafting Essentials

To survive the mid-game, you cannot rely on skills alone. You must craft armor and weapons to supplement your stats. The first step for any beginner is obtaining **Monster Leather**, which drops from basic mobs.

### The Smithing Process
To craft advanced gear, you need a **Smithing Bench**. This requires a total of **33 Iron** (31 for an Anvil and 2 for the Smithing Table itself). Once you have a bench, you can craft Monster Leather Armor, which actually evolves as you gain EP.

### Mining Magic Ore
For late-game Magisteel armor, you must head underground. Magic Ore Shards are found deep in the caves.
*   **Recommended Depth:** Search between Y-levels -25 and -31.
*   **Optimal Level:** Y-level **-29** is statistically the best height for Magic Ore.
*   **Tip:** Use a pickaxe with the **Fortune** enchantment to maximize your yield per vein.

## Advanced Progression: Mid to Late Game

As you approach the mid-game, focus on "Awakenings." These are specialized evolutions that provide massive stat multipliers. It is highly recommended to wait until you have at least **1 million EP** before attempting an Awakening to ensure you can handle the increased difficulty of late-game bosses.

### EP Farming Strategies
1.  **Enderman Farms:** Excellent for AFK farming if you have a stable setup in the End.
2.  **Sissie Hunting:** Found in broad ocean biomes, these mobs provide immense amounts of EP for active players.
3.  **Boss Raids:** Fighting bosses like the **Orc Disaster** or **Hinata** provides the largest chunks of EP and Souls but requires a coordinated team.

> **💡 Tip:** If you have a "Predatory" type skill, you can steal abilities from summoned Spirits or bosses to quickly expand your toolkit without relying on RNG.

## FAQ

**Q: Why didn't my skill upgrade during my last evolution?**

A: The most common reason is that the skill's mastery was not at 100%. The game only triggers an upgrade for fully mastered skills at the moment of racial evolution. Always double-check your UI before clicking the evolve button.

**Q: What is the fastest way to use this tensura incremental beginners guide to reach Demon Lord status?**

A: Focus on "Basic Skill" spamming to mastery your first set of abilities while farming mobs for 10,000 Souls. Once you have the souls and 100% mastery on your Unique Skill, evolve your race to unlock your first Ultimate Skill.

**Q: Where can I find the Orc Disaster boss?**

A: You must first find an Orc Lord and allow it to gain 200,000 EP. A common strategy is to feed it high-EP subordinates. Once it reaches the threshold, it will evolve into the Orc Disaster, allowing you to fight it for high-tier rewards.

**Q: Can I use Racial Skills to charge my Ultimate Meter?**

A: No. In the current 2026 version of Tensura Incremental, only Basic Skills contribute to the Ultimate Meter charge. Unique, Spirit, and Racial skills are for damage and utility but will not help you ready your Ultimate attack.
