## Core Stat Mechanics and Training

In Tensura Incremental, your power is derived from several key pillars. While traditional RPGs might focus solely on level, this game utilizes a multi-layered approach to stat growth. You must balance your active training with passive multipliers gained through race progression.

The primary stats usually include Strength, Agility, and Intelligence, but the most critical factor is your "Magicule" count or overall power rating. These stats are boosted by training equipment and environmental factors found within the various maps.

| Stat Type | Primary Benefit | Scaling Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | Increases physical damage and health | Manual training / Weights |
| **Agility** | Boosts attack speed and movement | Speed zones / Treadmills |
| **Intelligence** | Enhances skill damage and mana pool | Meditation / Magic circles |
| **Magicules** | Overall power multiplier | Global progression / Evolution |

> **💡 Tip:** Always look for training zones that offer a multiplier. Training in the starting area for too long is a common mistake that slows down your stat accumulation.

## Mastering Skills for Evolution

One of the most vital aspects of this **tensura incremental stat guide** is the concept of Skill Mastery. Unlike many other Roblox games where skills level up automatically as you use them, Tensura Incremental requires a specific focus on mastery to unlock the next tier of power.

According to veteran players, you should never attempt to evolve your race until your current skill mastery has reached 100%. This is a hard requirement that many beginners miss, leading to confusion when the "Evolve" button appears unresponsive or the stat gains are lower than expected.

### How to Upgrade Your Skills
1.  **Select an Active Skill:** Equip the skill you wish to master.
2.  **Usage and Grinding:** Use the skill repeatedly against training dummies or enemies in dungeons.
3.  **Monitor Mastery:** Check your skill menu to ensure the percentage is climbing.
4.  **Reach 100%:** Do not stop until the mastery bar is completely full.
5.  **Race Evolution:** Once mastery is capped, navigate to the race menu to trigger your evolution.

Each evolution represents a significant jump in your "stat ceiling," allowing you to reach higher numbers than previously possible. While it might be tempting to try and max out every single individual skill before moving on, the most efficient way to progress is to focus on the race upgrades themselves.

| Evolution Tier | Requirements | Major Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 (Base)** | Starting Race | Basic Stat Access |

## Prestige and Long-term Progression

Once you hit a certain threshold in your **tensura incremental stat guide** journey, you will encounter the Prestige system. This is a common mechanic in incremental games where you reset your current progress in exchange for permanent, powerful buffs that make your next run significantly faster.

Many players hesitate to prestige because they don't want to lose their hard-earned stats. However, the multipliers provided by the prestige system are essential for reaching the late-game content and tackling the highest-level dungeons.

### When to Prestige
Knowing when to reset is an art form. If you prestige too early, the bonuses won't be enough to justify the reset. If you wait too long, you are wasting time on slow stat gains. Follow these milestones:

*   **First Prestige:** Trigger this as soon as you have maxed your first major race upgrade and progress feels like it has hit a wall.
*   **Subsequent Prestiges:** Aim for a 10x increase in your prestige currency compared to your previous run.
*   **Dungeon Readiness:** Ensure you have collected the best possible items from your current dungeon tier before resetting, as items often persist or provide a significant head start.

## FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my race evolving even though I have high stats?**

A: Stats alone do not trigger evolution. As mentioned in this **tensura incremental stat guide**, you must ensure your skill mastery is at 100% and you have purchased the necessary race upgrades in the evolution menu.

**Q: Do I lose my items when I prestige?**

A: Generally, items obtained from dungeons are kept through prestige, though this can vary based on specific game updates. Always check the prestige confirmation screen for the most current rules regarding item retention.

**Q: What is the best stat to focus on for beginners?**

A: Strength and Health are usually the safest bets for beginners, as they allow you to survive longer in dungeons and clear early-game mobs more efficiently. However, once you unlock powerful skills, Intelligence becomes vital for maximizing your burst damage.

**Q: How often should I check my skill mastery?**

A: You should check it every time you finish a significant grinding session. Since 100% mastery is the gateway to race evolution, you don't want to spend hours training a skill that is already capped.
