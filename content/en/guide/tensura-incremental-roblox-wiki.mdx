## Skill Synthesis and the Great Sage

The Great Sage mechanic serves as your primary interface for managing abilities. In *Tensura: Incremental*, skills are categorized by rarity: Common, Rare, Unique, and Ultimate. By using the "Synthesis" tab, you can combine lower-tier skills to create more powerful versions.

1. **Collect Skill Shards** by defeating world bosses or completing daily quests.
2. **Access the Great Sage Menu** and select the "Synthesize" option.
3. **Combine five shards** of the same rarity for a 75% chance to upgrade to the next tier.
4. **Equip Ultimate Skills** to your hotbar to provide massive passive boosts to your incremental gains.

The synergy between skills is what separates top-tier players from the rest of the pack. For instance, pairing a "Regeneration" skill with a "Magic Consumption" reducer allows for near-infinite uptime on your most powerful active abilities.
