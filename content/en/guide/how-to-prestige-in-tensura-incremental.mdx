## Understanding the Prestige Requirements

Before you can reset your progress, you must meet specific criteria within the game world. There is often a misconception among the community that you need to achieve a "perfect" save before prestiging. In reality, the game only checks for a few specific flags related to your race's development.

To prestige, your primary focus should be on your **Race Upgrade**. While the game features dozens of skills and secondary attributes, these are secondary to your evolutionary line. You do not need to max out every single skill in your library. Instead, you must reach the final evolution stage of your current race and ensure that the race-specific upgrades are capped.

### Key Milestones for Prestige
| Requirement | Detail | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Race Upgrade** | Must be maxed out for your current tier | **High** |
| **Existence Points (EP)** | Required to trigger the evolution sequence | **Essential** |
| **Souls** | 10,000+ Souls typically required for late-stage evolution | **High** |
| **Skill Mastery** | Not required for basic prestige | **Low** |

> **💡 Tip:** Don't waste time grinding minor skills like "Resistance" or "Speed" to their absolute maximum if your goal is a fast prestige. Focus entirely on Existence Points (EP) to push your evolution forward.

## Evolution Tiers and Resource Costs

Navigating the evolution menu can be confusing for new players. Each race has a different path, but they all rely on the same two resources: Existence Points (EP) and Souls. EP is generally earned through combat and active play, while Souls are rarer drops found in specific dungeons or rewarded for completing major boss fights.

Understanding the scaling of these costs is vital for planning your prestige runs. If you know exactly how much EP you need for the next stage, you can avoid over-farming and move to the next prestige cycle immediately.

| Evolution Tier | EP Required (Approx) | Souls Required | Time Estimate (Solo) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 (Base)** | 1,000 | 0 | 5 Minutes |
| **Tier 2 (Mid)** | 15,000 | 500 | 15 Minutes |
| **Tier 3 (Advanced)** | 100,000 | 2,500 | 45 Minutes |
| **Tier 4 (Final)** | 500,000+ | 10,000 | 2+ Hours |

> **Warning:** Always check your Soul count before attempting the final evolution. If you trigger the evolution without enough Souls, you may find yourself stuck in a state where you cannot progress to the prestige screen.

## Optimizing Your Build for Prestige Runs

## FAQ

**Q: Will I lose all my items when I learn how to prestige in tensura incremental and reset?**

A: Most basic items and equipment are reset upon prestige to maintain game balance. However, certain "Legacy" items and permanent upgrades purchased with premium currency or earned through special achievements will persist across all prestige levels.

**Q: Why can't I see the prestige button even though I'm at a high level?**

A: The prestige button is tied specifically to your race evolution. You must reach the final form of your chosen race and ensure the "Race Upgrade" stat is maxed out. Being a high level in terms of raw stats is not enough if your evolution path is incomplete.

**Q: Is the Slime race better than the Human race for prestiging?**

A: While the Slime race is the most iconic, it often has more complex evolution requirements. For players focusing purely on speed, the Human race is currently recommended because it requires fewer resources to reach the final tier, making the prestige loop much faster.

**Q: How many souls do I actually need for a fast prestige?**

A: For most races, the 10,000 soul mark is the "sweet spot" that allows you to bypass the final evolution grind. While you can prestige with fewer in some specific builds, 10,000 ensures you won't hit a wall during the final stages of your race upgrade.
