## Resource Management: Souls and Existence Points

To sustain the power of a high-level **tensura: incremental race**, you must master the economy of the game. The 2026 update changed the health system, making it more expensive to maintain high HP pools. This makes the "Existence Points" (EP) grind even more vital.

### How to Farm Effectively
*   **Existence Points:** These are primarily earned through consistent training and participating in Raids. EP directly increases your Magicule capacity, which is the "fuel" for your ultimate skills.
*   **Souls:** Souls are gathered by defeating enemies. You will need a minimum of 10,000 for the final racial evolution step, but keeping a surplus is recommended for future skill upgrades.
*   **Prestige Strategy:** Only prestige when a 2x Bonus event is active. This doubles your chances of hitting the rare True Dragon race and speeds up the re-leveling process.

> **💡 Tip:** Focus on basic skill mastery early. Since only basic skills charge your ultimate meter, having them at high levels ensures you can trigger your "Lord" skills more frequently during long boss fights.

## Advanced Combat Strategies

As you progress with your chosen **tensura: incremental race**, combat becomes a dance of resource management. You cannot simply spam your strongest moves. Because Ultimate Skills require millions of Magicules, you must monitor your EP levels constantly.

If you find yourself running out of Magicules mid-fight, it is a sign that your EP is too low for your current race tier. Spend more time in the training zones or join lower-level raids to build up your base stats. Additionally, the new UI provides better visual cues for your ultimate meter—keep an eye on the glow effect around your skill icons to know exactly when your "Lord" skill is ready for deployment.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best tensura: incremental race for solo players?**

A: For solo progression, the **True Dragon** is the best due to its overwhelming stat bonuses. However, if you cannot hit the 0.25% drop rate, the **Demon Peer** is a fantastic alternative because its high Magicule capacity allows for more frequent skill usage without relying on a team.

**Q: Why isn't my ultimate meter charging?**

A: The ultimate meter only charges when you use **basic skills**. Using racial skills, spirit skills, or unique skills will not contribute to the meter. Ensure you have a few low-cooldown basic skills in your rotation to keep the meter moving.

**Q: Is the 10,000 soul cost for evolution a one-time payment?**

A: Yes, the 10,000 soul cost to unlock the Ultimate Skill slot via race evolution is a one-time fee per evolution tier. However, if you prestige or change your race, you may need to progress through the evolution tree again.

**Q: How do I get more Magicules quickly?**

A: Magicule capacity is tied to your Existence Points (EP). The fastest way to gain EP in 2026 is by participating in high-level Raids or using the AFK training zones if you have the appropriate multipliers active.
